## Overview

Awesome Urban Datasets provides a straightforward way to discover urban datasets that can be of interest to users in multiple domains: computer science (data analytics, data mining, visualization, machine learning), social science (urban planning, economics), climate science, and public health.

## Dataset Categories

### Transportation & Mobility
- Traffic flow data
- Public transit schedules and ridership
- Bike-sharing and micro-mobility data
- Parking availability
- Pedestrian movement patterns

### Environmental Data
- Air quality measurements
- Noise pollution levels
- Green space distribution
- Urban heat island effects
- Weather and climate data

### Infrastructure
- Building footprints and heights
- Road networks
- Utility infrastructure
- Points of interest (POI)
- Land use classification

### Socioeconomic Data
- Demographics and census data
- Economic indicators
- Crime statistics
- Health outcomes
- Education facilities

## Data Sources

Datasets are collected from:
- Government open data portals
- Research institutions
- Crowdsourced platforms
- Sensor networks
- Commercial data providers

## Applications

These datasets enable research and applications in:
- **Smart Cities**: Optimizing urban services and infrastructure
- **Urban Planning**: Evidence-based city development
- **Data Science**: Machine learning and predictive modeling
- **Visualization**: Creating interactive urban dashboards
- **Public Health**: Understanding health disparities and access
- **Climate Adaptation**: Planning for resilience

## Data Formats

Datasets are available in various formats including:
- GeoJSON and Shapefiles for spatial data
- CSV and JSON for tabular data
- APIs for real-time access
- Raster formats for satellite imagery

## Use Cases

- Traffic prediction and optimization
- Urban growth modeling
- Environmental monitoring
- Social equity analysis
- Emergency response planning

## Quality & Accessibility

All featured datasets are:
- Publicly accessible
- Well-documented
- Updated regularly (where applicable)
- Suitable for academic and commercial use
