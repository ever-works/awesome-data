## Overview

Awesome Workflow Engines catalogs open-source workflow orchestration systems that help teams design, execute, and monitor complex workflows and business processes across distributed systems.

## Features

- Workflow engines like Apache Airflow, Prefect, and Temporal
- Business process management (BPM) systems
- State machines and saga pattern implementations
- Event-driven workflow systems
- Microservices orchestration tools like Conductor and Cadence
- Visualization and monitoring dashboards
- Retry logic and error handling mechanisms
- Integration with cloud services and databases
- Scaling patterns for high-throughput workflows
- Examples and templates for common use cases
