## Overview

Awesome DeepBio catalogs deep learning applications and tools specifically designed for computational biology and biomedical research, from genomics to drug discovery.

## Features

- Genomics and sequence analysis models
- Protein structure prediction (AlphaFold and similar)
- Drug discovery and molecular property prediction
- Medical image analysis and diagnostics
- Single-cell analysis and transcriptomics
- Cancer genomics and precision medicine
- Systems biology and network analysis
- Deep learning frameworks for biology (DeepChem, BioBERT)
- Pre-trained models and datasets
- Research papers and implementations
