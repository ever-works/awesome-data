# Awesome Amazon Seller

A community-maintained, GitHub-hosted directory of tools, services, and educational resources for Amazon sellers.

**Website:** https://github.com/ScaleLeap/awesome-amazon-seller  
**Category:** Themed directories  
**Tags:** awesome-lists, ecommerce, business-tools

---

## Overview

Awesome Amazon Seller is an open, curated list that organizes useful resources for Amazon sellers into clear categories. It focuses on helping sellers find software, learning materials, and other external tools to manage and grow their businesses.

---

## Features

- **Curated resource directory**
  - Focused specifically on Amazon sellers and related ecommerce activities.
  - Emphasis on tools and resources that support running and scaling an Amazon business.

- **Organized contents**
  - **Software and Tools** – external applications and services for Amazon sellers (e.g., analytics, listing tools, operations, etc.).
  - **Podcasts** – audio resources related to Amazon selling, ecommerce strategies, and industry news.
  - **Blogs** – written content for education, tactics, and updates relevant to Amazon businesses.
  - **Other** – additional useful links and resources that don’t fit the above categories.

- **Open contribution model**
  - Public GitHub repository where anyone can suggest new tools or resources.
  - Documented **contribution guidelines** (`contributing.md`) describing how to add or update entries.
  - **Code of Conduct** to guide community behavior and maintain a respectful environment.

- **Standard Awesome List format**
  - Follows the general conventions of “Awesome” lists (https://awesome.re), making it easy to browse and understand.

- **Open license**
  - Published under **CC0 (Public Domain Dedication)**.
  - Content may be reused, modified, or redistributed without restriction, to the extent permitted by law.
