## Overview

Awesome Fintech is a comprehensive collection of open-source financial technology libraries and resources covering the entire spectrum of financial services from payments to banking infrastructure.

## Payment Integration

### Payment Gateways and Processors
- **Stripe**: Global technology company building economic infrastructure for the internet
- **PayPal**: Digital payment platform for online transactions
- **Adyen**: End-to-end payments platform
- **Square**: Financial services and payment processing
- **Braintree**: Payment platform owned by PayPal

### Payment Libraries
- PayPal integration libraries for multiple languages
- React Native payment integrations
- UPI (Unified Payments Interface) libraries

## Banking Infrastructure

### Core Banking
- **Apache Fineract**: Open-source core banking platform
- **Mifos**: Financial inclusion platform
- ISO8583 gateway implementations for banking/fintech POS systems

### Banking Standards
- **ISO 20022**: Universal financial industry message scheme
- **SWIFT**: Secure financial messaging services
- **PCI DSS**: Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard
