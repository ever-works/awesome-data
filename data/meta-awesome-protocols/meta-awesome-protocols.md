## Meta Awesome – Protocols

**Category:** Meta Directories  
**Website:** https://github.com/jepemo/meta-awesome/blob/master/doc/protocols.md  
**Source:** GitHub

### Overview
Meta Awesome – Protocols is a curated meta-directory that aggregates "Awesome"-style lists related to network protocols, communication standards, and associated tooling. It serves as a central index pointing to specialized collections of resources across the networking and protocols ecosystem.

### Features
- **Meta-directory of lists:** Aggregates multiple Awesome-style lists focused specifically on protocols.
- **Networking focus:** Covers network protocols and related communication mechanisms.
- **Standards coverage:** Includes lists related to communication standards, specifications, and reference documents.
- **Tooling references:** Points to resources and tools used to develop, test, debug, or analyze protocols.
- **Awesome-list ecosystem integration:** Organizes and links to existing Awesome lists rather than duplicating their content.
- **GitHub-hosted:** Maintained as a Markdown document in a public GitHub repository, making it easy to browse, clone, and contribute.
