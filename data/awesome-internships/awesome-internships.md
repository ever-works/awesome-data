# Awesome Internships

**Description**  
A curated, open-source list of resources for people looking for tech internships, including guidance on applications, CVs/resumes, interview preparation, and more.

**Website**  
https://github.com/lodthe/awesome-internships#readme

**Category**  
Themed Directories

**Tags**  
awesome-lists, startups, education

---

## Features

- **Curated tech internship resources**  
  Centralized collection aimed at candidates seeking internships in technology-related roles.

- **General internship guidance**  
  General information and tips for getting started with the tech internship search.

- **Where to apply**  
  Links and resources pointing to platforms, companies, or aggregators that list tech internship opportunities.

- **How to write a CV**  
  Guides and best practices focused on crafting effective CVs/resumes for tech internships.

- **CV examples**  
  Sample CVs and resumes that can be used as references or templates.

- **CV builders**  
  Tools and platforms that help users build or format their CVs.

- **CV analyzers**  
  Services that analyze CVs/resumes (e.g., for formatting, keywords, or clarity) to improve internship applications.

- **Feedback on your CV**  
  Resources where candidates can receive feedback or reviews on their CVs.

- **Employee referral resources**  
  Directories or platforms that help candidates find employee referrals for internship positions.

- **Practice platforms**  
  Sites and tools for practicing skills relevant to tech internships (e.g., coding challenges, technical exercises).

- **Mock interview platforms**  
  Services and tools for conducting or simulating interviews (technical or behavioral) to prepare for internship interview rounds.

- **Open-source and community-driven**  
  Public GitHub repository with contribution guidelines, allowing the community to propose new resources and improvements.

- **License information**  
  Includes a license file specifying terms of use and contribution.
