# Awesome Speaking

**Description:**  
A curated, open-source directory of resources, tools, and guides focused on public speaking, with an emphasis on tech talks and conference speaking.

**Website:** https://github.com/matteofigus/awesome-speaking#readme  
**Creator / Brand:** matteofigus  
**Category:** Themed directories  
**Tags:** awesome-lists, communication, productivity  
**License:** MIT

---

## Features

- **Curated public speaking resources**
  - Focus on talks in tech, conferences, and meetups.
  - Community-maintained list with contributions via GitHub.

- **Content types covered**
  - **Blog posts** – Articles and write-ups on improving public speaking and conference talks.
  - **Books** – Recommended reading for presentation skills, speaking craft, and communication.
  - **CFP (Call for Papers) resources** –
    - List of CFP sites and services.
    - Helps discover, track, and submit talk proposals to events.
  - **Clubs / Organizations** – Groups and communities that support practicing and improving public speaking.
  - **Videos** – Recorded talks, tutorials, and other visual learning resources.
  - **Websites** – Dedicated sites and platforms offering speaking tips, templates, or tools.
  - **Miscellaneous** – Additional useful material that doesn’t fit in the above categories.

- **Open contribution model**
  - Clear contributing guidelines (`contributing.md`) for adding or improving resources.
  - Contributions from multiple community members (GitHub contributors).

- **Repository structure and tooling**
  - README-based index of all resources.
  - MIT license for open reuse and adaptation.
  - CI configuration (`.travis.yml`) present for repository checks.
