## Overview

Awesome Legal Skills provides a comprehensive collection of skills and techniques for automating legal work, with particular focus on statutory interpretation, legal reasoning, and document analysis. This resource is valuable for legal professionals, compliance officers, and developers building legal AI applications.

## Features

- **Statutory Interpretation**: Methods for reading and interpreting laws
- **Canons of Construction**: Legal interpretation principles
- **Legislative Intent**: Understanding lawmaker objectives
- **Legal Research**: Automated case and statute research
- **Document Analysis**: Contract and legal document review
- **Compliance Automation**: Regulatory requirement mapping
- **Legal Drafting**: Template-based document generation
- **Case Law Analysis**: Precedent identification and application

### Canons of Construction
- **Ejusdem generis**: General follows specific
- **Expressio unius**: Expression of one excludes others
- **Noscitur a sociis**: Word meaning from context
- **In pari materia**: Related statutes read together
- **Harmonization**: Reconciling conflicting provisions
