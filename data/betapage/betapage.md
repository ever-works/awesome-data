## Overview

BetaPage is a startup launch platform that takes a more lenient approach than BetaList, providing immediate visibility and accessibility for submitted products at any stage of development.

## Key Features

- **Immediate Public Link**: Products receive accessible URLs right after submission
- **No Strict Approval**: More lenient acceptance criteria than BetaList
- **Any Stage Welcome**: Early-stage to mature products accepted
- **Newsletter Distribution**: 30,000-45,000 subscribers
- **Large Catalog**: 40,000+ listed products
