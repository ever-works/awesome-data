## Description
Meta Awesome - People is an Awesome-inspired meta directory that aggregates curated lists and Awesome repositories centered on notable people, contributors, and experts across diverse domains. It acts as a central index for people-focused Awesome lists, helping users discover influential individuals and communities in various fields.

## Features
- **Meta directory of people-focused Awesome lists**: Collects links to Awesome repositories that highlight notable people, contributors, and experts.
- **Curated focus**: Emphasizes curated, community-maintained lists rather than arbitrary collections.
- **Cross-domain coverage**: Includes people from multiple fields and disciplines (e.g., technology, open source, research, and other areas where Awesome lists exist).
- **Awesome ecosystem integration**: Follows the "Awesome" list style and structure, integrating into the broader Awesome lists ecosystem.
- **Community-driven updates**: Hosted on GitHub, allowing community contributions, suggestions, and improvements via pull requests.
- **Single entry point**: Serves as a starting hub to discover many different people-focused Awesome repositories from one place.

- **Free**: Public GitHub repository; accessible and usable at no cost.
