## Overview

The **Awesome Public Datasets – Economics Collection** is a curated subset of the broader Awesome Public Datasets project, focused specifically on economics-related data. It aggregates high‑quality, openly accessible datasets covering macroeconomic indicators, trade, productivity, corporate information, and long‑run historical series, aimed at researchers, data scientists, and policy analysts.

## Features

- **Economics-focused subset of Awesome Public Datasets**  
  Dedicated section of the Awesome Public Datasets meta‑collection concentrating on economic data sources.

- **Curated data sources**  
  Datasets selected and organized to highlight reputable, useful, and openly accessible economics data for analysis and research.

- **Macroeconomic indicators**  
  Links to data covering national and international macro indicators such as GDP, inflation, employment, interest rates, and other aggregate statistics (as provided by the underlying sources).

- **Trade statistics**  
  References to datasets that include international trade flows, imports/exports, tariffs, and related cross-border economic measures.

- **Productivity and growth data**  
  Sources for productivity metrics, output per worker, sectoral performance, and long‑run growth indicators.

- **Corporate and business registries**  
  Data sources that include information on companies, business registries, and corporate characteristics where available as open data.

- **Long-run historical series**  
  Collections of historical economic time series, often spanning many decades, suitable for longitudinal and historical economic research.

- **Open and publicly accessible datasets**  
  Focuses on datasets that are freely and publicly accessible (subject to each source’s terms of use), enabling reproducible research and open analysis.

- **Useful for multiple audiences**  
  Designed for use by economists, data scientists, policy analysts, students, and anyone needing structured economic data.

- **GitHub-based directory**  
  Hosted as a GitHub repository, making it easy to browse, contribute, and integrate with version-controlled workflows.

- **Part of a larger meta-collection**  
  Integrated into the broader Awesome Public Datasets project, providing a gateway to additional non‑economics datasets if needed.

## Pricing

- **Cost:** No pricing information is provided in the available content. The collection points to public/open datasets; access is generally free but may depend on each underlying data provider’s terms.
