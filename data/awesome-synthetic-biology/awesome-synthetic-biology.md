## Overview

Awesome Synthetic Biology brings together resources for the field of synthetic biology, which combines biology and engineering to design and construct new biological parts, devices, and systems.

## Features

- DNA sequence design and optimization tools
- Genetic circuit design automation (Cello CAD)
- Plasmid repositories like Addgene
- Protein engineering and molecular modeling software
- CRISPR and gene editing resources
- Microfluidics design suites like Neptune
- Bioinformatics libraries (Biopython, Bioperl)
- Lab automation and robotics
- Educational resources and courses
- Research papers and protocols
