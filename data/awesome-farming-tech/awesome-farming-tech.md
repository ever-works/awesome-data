## Overview

Awesome Farming Tech provides resources for building IoT monitoring systems for aquaponics and other agricultural applications, showcasing latest innovations in farming technology.

## Aquaponics Systems

### What is Aquaponics?
Combines fish farming (aquaculture) with soilless plant cultivation (hydroponics) in a symbiotic environment.

### Monitoring Needs
- Water temperature
- pH levels
- Dissolved oxygen
- Ammonia/nitrite/nitrate
- Water level
- Light intensity
