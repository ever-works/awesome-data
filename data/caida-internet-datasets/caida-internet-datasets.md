# CAIDA Internet Datasets

**Website:** http://www.caida.org/data/overview/

Collection of Internet measurement and topology datasets from CAIDA, used for large-scale Internet and networking research.

---

## Features
- Catalog of CAIDA datasets and related research resources
- Internet traffic traces and measurement data
- Internet topology and routing datasets
- Security-related Internet datasets
- Searchable catalog interface
- Includes associated media, papers, and software
- Provides “recipes” (usage examples/methods) for working with datasets
- Focused on contributions to the scientific and Internet research community
