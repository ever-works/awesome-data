## Overview

This community-focused Awesome Synthetic Biology list encourages contributions from researchers and practitioners to build a comprehensive, up-to-date resource for the field.

## Features

- Open collaboration and community contributions
- Research papers and publications
- Conference proceedings and presentations
- Startup companies and commercial applications
- DIY biology resources and community labs
- Regulatory frameworks and guidelines
- Ethical considerations in synthetic biology
- Educational curricula and teaching materials
- Industry partnerships and funding opportunities
- Job boards and career resources

## Pricing

Free and open-source.
