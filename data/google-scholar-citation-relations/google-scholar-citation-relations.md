# Google Scholar Citation Relations

Research dataset of citation relationships between scholarly works, extracted from Google Scholar and listed in the Awesome Public Datasets project.

- **Category:** Datasets
- **Use Cases:** Citation network analysis, bibliometrics, scientometrics, research impact studies, network science experiments.

## Features

- Dataset of **citation relationships** between academic publications collected from Google Scholar.
- Structured to support **citation network construction** (nodes as papers, edges as citation links).
- Suitable for **bibliometric** and **scientometric analyses** (e.g., citation counts, co-citation, influence measures).
- Applicable to **social network analysis** of scholarly communication and knowledge diffusion.
- Listed within a curated, topic-centric collection of **public research datasets** (Awesome Public Datasets).
