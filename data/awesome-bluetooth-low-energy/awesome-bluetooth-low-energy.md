# Awesome Bluetooth Low Energy

**Type:** Themed directory / curated resource list  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** IoT, hardware, awesome-lists

A collaboratively maintained, GitHub-hosted "Awesome" list focused on **Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)** development. It aggregates libraries, tools, documentation, and learning resources for building BLE applications across hardware and mobile platforms.

---

## Features

### Scope
- Curated, categorized list of **BLE development resources**.
- Covers **multiple platforms and device types**, including:
  - Hardware (Arduino, ESP, Nordic, Raspberry Pi)
  - Mobile (Android, iOS, Flutter, KMM)
  - Web
- Includes **documentation, frameworks, libraries, tools, and security-related content** for BLE.

### Documentation Resources
Official and high‑quality technical references for BLE, including:
- **Android Bluetooth Low Energy** – Overview of BLE support and APIs on Android.
- **Developing a Bluetooth Low Energy Application (TI)** – TI’s guide for building BLE applications on their platforms.
- **Getting Started with Bluetooth Low Energy (Adafruit)** – Introductory yet comprehensive BLE development guide.
- **Intro to Bluetooth Generic Attribute Profile (GATT)** – Fundamentals of GATT, the core BLE data model.
- **Intro to Bluetooth Low Energy (Bluetooth SIG)** – Official overview of BLE basics and concepts.
- **IoT for Beginners (Microsoft)** – 12‑week, 24‑lesson IoT curriculum that includes BLE‑relevant concepts.
- **nRF51 SDK (Nordic Semiconductor)** – SDK for BLE development on Nordic nRF51 chips.
- **RN4870/71 BLE Module User’s Guide (Microchip)** – Technical guide for Microchip’s RN4870/71 BLE modules.
- **Bluetooth Low Energy on Wikipedia** – General reference on BLE concepts and history.

### Frameworks
Frameworks useful for building BLE peripherals and connected devices:
- **Matter (Project CHIP / connectedhomeip)** – Standard for interoperable connected home / IoT devices, with BLE used in commissioning and communication flows.
- **Zephyr RTOS** – Real-time operating system with BLE stack support for embedded systems and IoT hardware.
