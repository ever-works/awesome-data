## Overview

Awesome Founder Communities brings together the most valuable online spaces where entrepreneurs can connect with peers, find mentorship, share experiences, and grow their businesses together.

## Features

- Communities like Indie Hackers for profitable internet business founders
- Y Combinator Startup School and alumni networks
- Industry-specific founder groups
- Local and regional founder meetups
- Women in Tech and diversity-focused communities
- Slack and Discord channels for real-time collaboration
- Forum-based communities for async discussions
- Mastermind groups and accountability partners
- Office hours and mentorship programs
- Events, workshops, and networking opportunities

## Pricing

Free and open-source.
