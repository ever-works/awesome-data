# Awesome Corda

**Website:** https://github.com/chainstack/awesome-corda#readme  
**Category:** Themed directories  
**Tags:** awesome-lists, blockchain, business

## Overview
Awesome Corda is a curated, Awesome-style directory of libraries, tools, tutorials, and other learning resources for the Corda open source blockchain platform for business.

## Features
- **Curated resource list for Corda**
  - Focused on the Corda open source blockchain platform for business use cases.
  - Organized as an Awesome-style list for easy discovery.

- **Structured categories** (as indicated in the repository):
  - **Documentation** – official and community-written documentation resources.
  - **Programming** – code examples, SDK-related resources, and development materials.
  - **Tools** – utilities, frameworks, and supporting software for building and operating on Corda.
  - **Tutorials** – step-by-step guides and how-tos for learning Corda and building solutions.
  - **Learning and online courses** – courses and learning programs related to Corda.
  - **Videos** – recorded talks, presentations, and educational videos.
  - **CorDapps** – examples and references of CorDapps to try out or get inspiration from.
  - **Networks** – information about Corda-related networks.
  - **Community** – links to Corda community channels and resources.
  - **Misc** – additional Corda-related resources that don’t fit other categories.
  - **Official** – official Corda and R3 resources.

- **CorDapp discovery**
  - List of CorDapps to explore and learn from.
  - Encourages exploration of the official [CorDapp marketplace](https://marketplace.r3.com/).

- **Open contribution model**
  - Public contribution guidelines (`CONTRIBUTING.md`).
  - Code of conduct for community interactions.
  - Pull requests explicitly welcomed to expand and refine the list.

- **Open-source licensing**
  - Distributed under an open-source license (`LICENSE.md` in the repository).

## Pricing
- Not applicable. Awesome Corda is an open, GitHub-hosted curated list and is free to access and contribute to.
