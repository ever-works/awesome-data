# Awesome Electronics

A curated, community-maintained directory of electronics resources, tools, and projects for electronic engineers and hobbyists.

**Website:** https://github.com/kitspace/awesome-electronics

---

## Overview

Awesome Electronics is an open-source, GitHub-hosted "awesome list" focused on electronic engineering. It aggregates links to high-quality websites, services, software, tools, and learning materials relevant to both professionals and hobbyists working with electronic circuits.

---

## Features

### General
- Curated list format maintained in a public GitHub repository
- Focus on electronic engineering (low-power DC circuits, but applicable across EE/embedded domains)
- Resources applicable to both professionals and hobbyists
- Contribution guidelines available via `contributing.md` for community submissions and updates

### Learning Resources
- **Technical tutorials**: Practical guides and how-tos for building and understanding electronic circuits
- **Courses**: Structured learning resources and course-style materials
- **Theory**: References covering fundamental and advanced electronic engineering concepts
- **University course archives**: Links to archived academic course material relevant to EE

### Documentation
- Centralized links to datasheets, reference manuals, standards, and other documentation sources for electronics design

### Simulation Tools
- **Analog and mixed-signal circuit simulators**: Tools for simulating analog and mixed-signal circuits
- **Verilog HDL simulators**: Tools for simulating hardware designs described in Verilog

### PCB & EDA Tooling
- **Gerber viewers**
  - Online Gerber viewing tools
  - Installable (desktop) Gerber viewers
- **Free EDA packages**: No-cost electronic design automation suites
- **Paid EDA packages**: Commercial EDA tools
- **CAD-specific resources**
  - KiCad-related tools, libraries, and extensions
  - Eagle-related resources
  - Altium-related resources, including footprint and symbol library formats such as `.IntLib` and `.PcbLib`

### Manufacturing & Components
- **PCB batching services**: Fabrication services suitable for prototyping and small-batch PCB production
- **Part search engines**: Tools to search for electronic components across distributors and manufacturers

### Project & Inventory Management
- **Project sharing platforms**: Sites for publishing, discovering, and collaborating on electronics projects
- **Inventory management and purchasing**: Tools to manage component stock and streamline ordering

### Software & Tools
- **Miscellaneous software projects**: Utilities and applications helpful in electronic design, analysis, or workflow automation

### Hardware & Retail
- **Development board retailers**: Sources for purchasing development boards and related hardware platforms

### Community & Media
- **Blogs**: Curated blogs focused on electronics design, practice, and industry topics
- **Forums**
  - Discussion forums for general electronics topics
  - Help-focused forums for troubleshooting and Q&A
- **Podcasts**: Audio shows centered on electronics, embedded systems, and related topics
- **Videos**: Video channels and series covering tutorials, theory, teardowns, and project walkthroughs
