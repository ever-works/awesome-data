## Overview

Awesome Robotics Projects is a collection of innovative and accessible robotics projects, emphasizing open-source and affordable platforms.

## Features

- **Open Source Robots**: Fully open hardware and software designs
- **Affordable Platforms**: Budget-friendly robotics kits and components
- **Educational Robots**: Platforms for learning robotics
- **Research Projects**: Cutting-edge academic robotics
- **DIY Robots**: Home-built and maker-friendly designs
- **Social Robots**: Human interaction and companionship
- **Mobile Platforms**: Wheeled, legged, and flying robots
- **Manipulators**: Robotic arms and grippers
