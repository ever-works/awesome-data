# Awesome Public Datasets – Government: Lexington, KY

**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** government, datasets, open-data

Awesome Public Datasets entry for Lexington, Kentucky’s open data portal, providing access to city government datasets for civic, analytic, and research use.

## Overview
Lexington’s data hub is a collaborative open data portal created by the Lexington-Fayette Urban County Government (LFUCG) and the local community. It serves as a central access point for government-related public datasets.

## Features
- Centralized “data hub” for Lexington, KY public data
- Focus on city government datasets and civic information
- Intended for research, analysis, and community applications
- Collaborative initiative between LFUCG and the community
- Part of the broader Awesome Public Datasets collection (curated directory entry rather than a standalone product)

## Links
