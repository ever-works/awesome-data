## Overview

Awesome Cybersecurity Projects provides hands-on project ideas and structured career paths for aspiring cybersecurity professionals, with practical exercises and certification roadmaps.

## Features

- 60 hands-on cybersecurity project ideas
- 10 structured career paths (SOC Analyst, Pentester, etc.)
- Certification roadmaps (CompTIA, CISSP, OSCP)
- Home lab setup guides
- CTF challenges and platforms
- Bug bounty program resources
- Security tool tutorials and implementations
- Real-world scenario simulations
- Portfolio building guidance
- Job search and interview preparation

## Pricing

Free and open-source.
