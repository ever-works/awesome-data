## Overview

Awesome Newsletter Tools is a collection of awesome email newsletter tools, platforms, media, and software for publishers, creators, and marketers.

## Self-Hosted Platforms

- **Sendy** - Self-hosted newsletter application that uses Amazon SES
- **SendPortal** - Open-source self-hosted email marketing solution
- **Mautic** - Open-source marketing automation
- **listmonk** - High-performance newsletter manager

## Commercial Newsletter Platforms

- Substack - Newsletter platform with built-in payments
- ConvertKit - Email marketing for creators
- Mailchimp - All-in-one marketing platform
- Buttondown - Minimal newsletter tool
