## Overview

Awesome Digital Twins collects resources on digital twin technology, which creates virtual representations of physical objects or systems for monitoring, simulation, and optimization.

## What are Digital Twins?

Digital twins are virtual replicas of physical entities that:
- Mirror real-world behavior
- Update in real-time
- Enable simulation and prediction
- Support decision-making
- Optimize operations
