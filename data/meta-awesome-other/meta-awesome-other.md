## Overview
meta-awesome – Other is a general-purpose "catch‑all" section within the broader *meta-awesome* project. It collects and organizes links to "awesome" lists that don’t fit cleanly into more specific or predefined categories, serving as a directory of miscellaneous curated lists.

## Features
- **General-purpose category** for awesome lists that don’t belong in existing topical sections.
- **Part of the meta-awesome ecosystem**, which focuses on directories of curated lists.
- **Directory-of-directories role**, linking out to other awesome-style collections rather than hosting all content directly.
- **Supports miscellaneous topics**, enabling inclusion of niche, emerging, or cross‑disciplinary lists.
- **Curated organization**, helping users discover awesome lists that might otherwise be hard to categorize or find.
- **GitHub-hosted documentation** (via `doc/other.md`), making it accessible and version-controlled alongside the rest of the project.
