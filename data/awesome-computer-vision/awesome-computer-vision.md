# Awesome Computer Vision

**Description**  
A curated, community-maintained directory of computer vision resources, including books, courses, research papers, software libraries, datasets, pre-trained models, and tutorials.

**Source**  
<https://github.com/jbhuang0604/awesome-computer-vision#readme>

**Tags**

## Features

### Scope & Focus
- Curated list dedicated to computer vision and closely related fields.  
- Covers both foundational theory and practical implementations.  
- Inspired by and follows the general style of the broader “Awesome” lists ecosystem.

### People & Academic Genealogy
- Separate `people.md` directory listing researchers in computer vision.  
- Includes academic genealogy information (who advised whom, etc.).

### Resource Categories (Table of Contents)

#### Awesome Lists
- Links to other “awesome” lists that are relevant to computer vision, machine learning, and related domains.

#### Books
- **Computer Vision** – Core vision textbooks and reference books.  
- **OpenCV Programming** – Books focused on practical computer vision using OpenCV.  
- **Machine Learning** – Books on ML methods used in vision.  
- **Fundamentals** – Foundational math, optimization, and related background material.

#### Courses
- **Computer Vision** – University and online courses focused on vision.  
- **Computational Photography** – Courses on imaging, photography, and related computation.  
- **Machine Learning and Statistical Learning** – Courses on ML/SL relevant to vision.  
- **Optimization** – Courses covering optimization techniques used in vision algorithms.

#### Papers
- **Conference papers on the web** – Links to major CV conference papers (e.g., CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, etc., via the original repo).  
- **Survey Papers** – Survey and review articles summarizing subfields of computer vision.

#### Pre-trained Computer Vision Models
- Collection of links to pre-trained models for common tasks (e.g., classification, detection, segmentation), hosted in external repositories.

#### Tutorials and Talks
- **Computer Vision** – General tutorials and intro/advanced talks.  
- **Recent Conference Talks** – Talks from recent vision conferences.  
- **3D Computer Vision** – Tutorials and lectures on 3D reconstruction, geometry, etc.  
- **Internet Vision** – Vision problems related to web-scale images and video.  
- **Computational Photography** – Talks on imaging, photo enhancement, and related topics.  
- **Learning and Vision** – Material at the intersection of machine learning and vision.  
- **Object Recognition** – Tutorials focused on recognition and categorization.  
- **Graphical Models** – Talks that use probabilistic/graphical models for vision tasks.  
- **Machine Learning** – ML talks relevant to computer vision.  
- **Optimization** – Talks on optimization methods frequently used in vision.  
- **Deep Learning** – Tutorials and talks about deep learning for vision.

#### Software
A large section of software and libraries organized by task:

- **Annotation tools** – Tools for labeling images and videos (e.g., bounding boxes, masks).  
- **External Resource Links** – Aggregated external portals and resource hubs.  
- **General Purpose Computer Vision Library** – Multi-purpose vision libraries (e.g., OpenCV and similar).  
- **Multiple-view Computer Vision** – Tools for multi-view geometry, structure-from-motion, 3D reconstruction.  
- **Feature Detection and Extraction** – Libraries for keypoints, descriptors, and local features.  
- **High Dynamic Range Imaging** – Tools for HDR image creation and processing.  
- **Semantic Segmentation** – Libraries and frameworks implementing semantic segmentation methods.  
- **Low-level Vision** – Algorithms for early vision tasks and image restoration, including:
  - **Stereo Vision** – Stereo matching and depth estimation.  
  - **Optical Flow** – Motion estimation between frames.  
  - **Image Denoising** – E.g., BM3D, KSVD and related denoising methods.  
  - **Super-resolution** – Single and multi-image super-resolution algorithms.  
  - **Image Deblurring** – Including:  
    - Non-blind deconvolution  
    - Blind deconvolution  
    - Non-uniform deblurring  
  - **Image Completion** – Inpainting and hole-filling methods.  
  - **Image Retargeting** – Content-aware resizing techniques.  
  - **Alpha Matting** – Foreground–background separation and matting.  
  - **Image Pyramid** – Multi-scale image representation tools.  
  - **Edge-preserving image processing** – Filters and methods that preserve edges while smoothing.

- **Intrinsic Images** – Methods and code for intrinsic image decomposition.  
- **Contour Detection and Image Segmentation** – Algorithms for edges, boundaries, and segmentation.  
- **Interactive Image Segmentation** – User-guided segmentation tools.  
- **Video Segmentation** – Temporal segmentation of video content.

- **Camera Calibration** – Software for calibrating cameras and lenses.  
- **Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM)** – Resources around visual SLAM, including:
  - **SLAM community** – Links to community resources.  
  - **Tracking/Odometry** – Visual odometry implementations.  
  - **Graph Optimization** – Back-end optimization libraries for SLAM.  
  - **Loop Closure** – Algorithms for detecting revisited places.  
  - **Localization & Mapping** – Libraries addressing localization and mapping subproblems.

- **Single-view Spatial Understanding** – Methods inferring 3D structure from single images.  
- **Object Detection** – Libraries and frameworks for object detection tasks.  
- **Nearest Neighbor Search** – Approximate and exact nearest-neighbor methods:
  - **General purpose nearest neighbor search** – Libraries for generic high-dimensional search.  
  - **Nearest Neighbor Field Estimation** – Methods for dense correspondences (e.g., patch-based matching).  
- **Visual Tracking** – Trackers for objects in video.  
- **Saliency Detection** – (Truncated in provided content but present in repo) tools for visual saliency.
