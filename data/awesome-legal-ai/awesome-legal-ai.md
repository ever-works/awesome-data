## Overview

Awesome Legal AI Resources aims to collect and curate comprehensive resources related to Legal AI, including datasets, research papers, tools, and other useful materials for researchers, developers, and anyone interested in the intersection of law and artificial intelligence.

## Key Categories

- **Legal AI Datasets** - Curated collections of legal data for training AI models
- **Research Papers** - Academic publications on legal artificial intelligence
- **AI Models** - Pre-trained models for legal applications
- **Tools & Frameworks** - Software for building legal AI systems
- **Intelligent Justice Systems** - Resources for AI-powered legal decision support
