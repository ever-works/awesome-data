## Overview
The MNIST database of handwritten digits is a classic benchmark dataset curated by Yann LeCun. It is widely used in machine learning and computer vision research for training and evaluating algorithms on handwritten digit recognition tasks. It is also listed in the broader "awesome" ecosystem of curated datasets, particularly under image processing.
