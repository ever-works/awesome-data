# Discogs Monthly Data

![Discogs Logo](https://st.discogs.com/4dfd2821c2b9d4b83c90908cb7d58c57b8a0e1a8/images/discogs-logo.svg)

Regularly published dumps of the Discogs music database, providing structured data on releases, artists, labels, and master releases, suitable for analysis, recommendation systems, graph modeling, and other machine learning or research tasks.

**Website:** http://data.discogs.com/

---

## Features

- **Monthly data dumps**  
  - Updated regularly with the latest Discogs database content.

- **Comprehensive music metadata**  
  - Release data  
  - Artist data  
  - Label data  
  - Master release data

- **XML format**  
  - All dumps provided in XML.  
  - Structured and machine-readable.  
  - Formatted according to the official Discogs API specification: http://www.discogs.com/developers/

- **License / Usage rights**  
  - Distributed under the **CC0 (No Rights Reserved)** license.  
  - Data can be freely used, modified, and redistributed, including for commercial and research purposes, subject to CC0 terms.

- **Use cases**  
  - Recommendation systems for music.  
  - Graph and network analysis of artists, labels, and releases.  
  - Machine learning and data mining on music metadata.  
  - Academic and industry research on music catalogs and discographies.
