## Overview

Tech Interview Handbook is one of the most comprehensive and popular resources for technical interview preparation, created by yangshun, the author of the famous Blind 75 LeetCode list.

## Complete Interview Lifecycle Coverage

### Before the Interview

- **Resume Preparation**: Crafting an effective software engineering resume
- **Application Strategy**: Where and how to apply
- **Networking**: Leveraging referrals and connections
- **Portfolio Building**: Showcasing your projects

### During the Interview

- **Coding Interviews**: Algorithms, data structures, problem-solving strategies
- **System Design**: Scalability, architecture patterns, distributed systems
- **Behavioral Interviews**: STAR method, common questions, storytelling
- **Domain-Specific**: Front-end, back-end, mobile, data engineering
- **Live Coding**: Best practices for pair programming and live sessions

### After the Interview

- **Offer Evaluation**: Comparing compensation packages
- **Salary Negotiation**: Proven strategies and tactics
- **Decision Making**: Choosing between multiple offers
- **Accepting/Declining**: Professional communication
