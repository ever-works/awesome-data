## Overview

NoDesk is a free remote job board providing listings across multiple categories including technology, creative, support, and startup roles.

## Key Features

- Completely free for job seekers
- Multiple job categories
- Remote-first companies
- Regular updates
- Clean interface

## Categories

- Technology and development
- Creative and design
- Customer support
- Startup roles
- Marketing and sales

## 2026 Positioning

Listed among high-value free remote job platforms alongside We Work Remotely, RemoteOK, Remote.co, and Remotive.

## Target Audience

Remote job seekers across multiple industries looking for free job board access.

## Pricing

Free for job seekers.
