# Twitter Data for Online Reputation Management

**Category:** Datasets  
**Brand:** replab-uned-nlp  
**Source:** [Awesome Public Datasets](https://project-awesome.org/awesomedata/awesome-public-datasets)

## Overview
Twitter Data for Online Reputation Management is a public Twitter dataset released for the RepLab 2013 evaluation campaign. It is designed as a benchmark collection for research on online reputation management in social media.

## Features
- **Domain focus:** Online reputation management and brand/entity monitoring on Twitter.
- **Platform:** Twitter posts (tweets) and related metadata.
- **Benchmark status:** Included in the Awesome Public Datasets collection as a reference/benchmark social media dataset.
- **Evaluation use:** Originally created for the RepLab 2013 shared task on online reputation management.
- **Task suitability:** Useful for tasks such as entity-centric tweet classification, reputation analysis, and related NLP experiments on social media.
- **Research orientation:** Intended for academic and experimental use in natural language processing and social media mining.

## Use Cases
- Training and evaluating models for online reputation monitoring.
- Studying brand or entity perception over Twitter.
- Benchmarking NLP methods on social media text.
