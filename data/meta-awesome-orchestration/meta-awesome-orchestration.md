**Category:** Meta-directories

## Overview
meta-awesome – Orchestration is a curated meta-directory that indexes “awesome lists” focused on orchestration platforms, schedulers, and automation frameworks. It aggregates links to specialized lists so developers and DevOps engineers can more easily discover tools and resources related to orchestration and automation.

## Features
- **Meta-index of awesome lists**
  - Collects and organizes third‑party “awesome” lists about orchestration technologies.
  - Focuses on platforms, schedulers, and automation frameworks in the DevOps ecosystem.

- **Category-based organization**
  - Groups resources under the broader orchestration theme for easier navigation.
  - Fits within the larger `meta-awesome` structure of categorized meta-lists.

- **Developer- and DevOps-focused scope**
  - Targets workflows involving orchestration, scheduling, and automation.
  - Useful for exploring tools and frameworks relevant to CI/CD and broader DevOps practices.
