## Overview

Awesome AI Music Generation is a curated collection of resources at the intersection of artificial intelligence and music creation, focusing on generative models and machine learning for composition and sound synthesis.

## AI Music Tools & Platforms

### Google Magenta
- Research project exploring machine learning in art and music
- Music generation models and creative tools
- NSynth - Neural audio synthesis
- MusicVAE - Variational autoencoder for music
- Performance RNN for melody generation

### Sound Synthesis

**NSynth Super**
- Neural network synthesizer from Google Magenta
- Enables exploration of 100,000+ sounds
- Real-time sound morphing
- Hardware and software implementations

**SuperCollider**
- Audio server and programming language
- Sound synthesis platform
- Algorithmic composition IDE
- Extensible architecture
