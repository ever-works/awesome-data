# Awesome Actions on Google

**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Brand:** Google Assistant  
**Source:** <https://github.com/ravirupareliya/awesome-actions-on-google#readme>

A curated, community-maintained directory of libraries, samples, and learning resources for building conversational experiences with Actions on Google and the Google Assistant.

![Awesome Actions on Google](https://repository-images.githubusercontent.com/99739594/0e2d5f80-6f45-11ea-9a11-7a1f5ff82ec3)

---

## Features

- **Curated “awesome” list**
  - Focused specifically on Actions on Google (AoG) / Google Assistant development.
  - Collects high‑quality, community‑vetted resources rather than raw search results.

- **Structured content sections** (as headings in the repository):
  - **Documents** – Official or community documentation and written guides related to Actions on Google.
  - **Blogs** – Articles and posts covering tips, tutorials, and insights about building conversational actions.
  - **Videos** – Video tutorials, talks, and demos for AoG development.
  - **Codelabs / Courses** – Step‑by‑step, hands‑on labs and longer‑form courses for learning AoG.
  - **Podcasts** – Audio content discussing conversational AI and Google Assistant ecosystem topics.
  - **Open source code** – Sample projects, libraries, and other code resources for implementing Actions.
  - **People to follow** – Notable developers or community members active in the AoG space.
  - **Applications** – Example Actions / applications built on Google Assistant.

- **Ecosystem focus**
  - Targets **conversational AI** and **Google Cloud / Google APIs** as they relate to Google Assistant and Actions on Google.

- **Open contribution model**
  - Public contribution guidelines (`contributing.md`) describe how to add or update resources.
  - Encourages community participation to keep the list up‑to‑date and comprehensive.

- **Open license**
  - Licensed under **CC0 (Public Domain Dedication)**, allowing reuse and adaptation of the list contents without restriction.
