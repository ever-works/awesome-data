## Overview
Awesome JMeter is a curated, community-maintained list of resources for Apache JMeter. It aggregates tools, plugins, integrations, and learning materials related to load testing and performance measurement with JMeter.

## Features
- **Curated resource collection** focused on Apache JMeter and its ecosystem.
- **Coverage of multiple aspects of JMeter usage**, including:
  - Plugins and extensions for JMeter
  - Integrations with other tools and platforms
  - Load testing and performance testing techniques
  - DevOps and CI/CD practices involving JMeter
