# Awesome Symfony Education

A curated directory of educational Symfony resources focused on articles, series, and books (not bundles).

**Website:** https://github.com/pehapkari/awesome-symfony-education#readme  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** symfony, education, web-development

---

## Description
Awesome Symfony Education is a community-maintained list of learning materials for the Symfony PHP framework. It aggregates written and video content to help developers of different levels deepen their Symfony knowledge.

---

## Features
- **Curated Symfony resources**
  - Focus on education: articles, multi-part series, and books
  - Excludes bundle listings (those are covered in a separate list)

- **Topical organization of content**
  - Single Action Controller
  - Tutorials and Video Courses
  - Series for beginners
  - Dependency Injection
  - EventDispatcher
  - Security
  - Forms
  - Doctrine
  - Translations
  - Bundles (educational content related to bundles)
  - Decoupling
  - Legacy applications
  - Performance
  - Domain-Driven Design (DDD)
  - General Symfony topics
  - Books

- **Beginner-friendly and advanced coverage**
  - Dedicated section for beginner series
  - More specialized topics like DDD, legacy code, and performance

- **Open and community-driven**
  - Hosted as a public GitHub repository
  - Uses CI (Travis) to maintain list consistency

- **License and reuse**
  - Released under CC0 (public domain dedication)
  - Copyright and related rights waived by the author for maximum reusability
