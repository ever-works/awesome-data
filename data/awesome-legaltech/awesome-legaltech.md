## Overview

Awesome LegalTech is a comprehensive curated list of legal technology resources, tools, and platforms that help legal professionals leverage technology for research, document automation, compliance, and legal AI applications.

## Key Categories

- **Open Source Platforms** - Free and open source legal tech software
- **AI Models** - Machine learning models designed for legal applications
- **Legal Research Tools** - Platforms for case law and statute research
- **Document Automation** - Tools for generating and managing legal documents
- **Compliance Tools** - Software for regulatory compliance and risk management
- **Legal Datasets** - Curated datasets for legal AI and research
- **MCP Servers** - Model Context Protocol servers for legal applications

## Featured Resources

- Legal AI models and natural language processing tools
- Document review and contract analysis platforms
- Legal research databases and APIs
- Practice management software
- E-discovery and litigation support tools

## Use Cases

- Automating contract review and analysis
- Legal research and case law discovery
- Regulatory compliance monitoring
- Document generation and management
- Legal analytics and prediction
