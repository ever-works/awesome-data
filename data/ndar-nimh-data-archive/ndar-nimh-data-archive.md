## NDAR (NIMH Data Archive)

**Description**  
The National Database for Autism Research (NDAR), now part of the NIMH Data Archive, is a large-scale repository for data related to autism spectrum disorder and related conditions.

### Features
- Centralized repository for autism spectrum disorder (ASD) research data.
- Part of the broader NIMH Data Archive infrastructure.
- Focus on data related to ASD and related neurodevelopmental conditions.
- Supports large-scale data sharing for research and analysis.
