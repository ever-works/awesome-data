## AIcrowd Competitions

**Website:** https://www.aicrowd.com/

AIcrowd Competitions is a platform that hosts a variety of AI and machine learning challenges, focused on applying crowdsourced intelligence to real-world problems. It provides curated datasets and public leaderboards to support both researchers and practitioners.

### Features
- Wide range of AI and machine learning competitions
- Focus on solving real-world problems through crowdsourcing
- Curated datasets for different tasks and domains
- Public leaderboards to track and compare solutions
- Suitable for researchers, practitioners, and enthusiasts
