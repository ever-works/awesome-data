## Overview

The Remote Freelancer is a comprehensive, community-maintained directory of platforms and resources for finding remote freelance work in tech.

## Features

- **Job Boards**: Curated list of platforms for finding freelance opportunities
- **Contract Work**: Resources for short-term and long-term contracts
- **Niche Platforms**: Specialized job boards for specific technologies or roles
- **Global Opportunities**: International remote work platforms
- **Developer-Focused**: Emphasis on software development opportunities
- **Design Work**: Resources for web and UI/UX designers
- **Community Verified**: Platforms vetted by the freelance community

## Categories

- General freelance marketplaces
