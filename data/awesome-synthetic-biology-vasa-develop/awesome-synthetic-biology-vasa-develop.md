## Overview

This Awesome Synthetic Biology list focuses on practical tools and platforms for designing, building, and testing biological systems, from DNA synthesis to microfluidic devices.

## Features

- Addgene nonprofit plasmid repository
- Neptune end-to-end microfluidic design suite
- DNA synthesis and assembly tools
- Genetic part registries and databases
- Computational modeling platforms
- Laboratory automation systems
- Bioprinting and biofabrication tools
- Metabolic engineering resources
- Biosafety and bioethics guidelines
- Educational programs and courses

## Pricing

Free and open-source.
