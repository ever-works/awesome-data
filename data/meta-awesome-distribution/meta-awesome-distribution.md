## Overview
Meta Awesome – Distribution is a curated meta-index of “awesome” lists related to software and package distribution. It aggregates and references existing awesome lists that focus on distribution channels, packaging formats, and artifact delivery, acting as a directory of directories for this domain.

## Features
- **Meta index of awesome lists**: Collects and links to multiple awesome lists instead of projects directly.
- **Focus on distribution topics**:
  - Software distribution channels
  - Package distribution ecosystems
  - Artifact delivery mechanisms
- **Packaging formats coverage**: References lists about various package and artifact formats (e.g., language-specific and cross-platform packaging systems).  
- **Distribution channels coverage**: Includes lists that focus on registries, repositories, app stores, and other delivery channels.
- **Artifact delivery tooling**: Points to awesome lists covering tools and services for building, publishing, and delivering artifacts.
- **Directory-of-directories structure**: Organized as a higher-level catalog to help discover the right awesome list for a specific distribution concern.
- **Community-maintained (GitHub)**: Hosted on GitHub, allowing contributions, issues, and updates via pull requests.

## Use Cases
- Finding specialized awesome lists about software/package distribution.
- Exploring packaging formats and distribution ecosystems from a single entry point.
- Using as a research starting point for building or improving distribution pipelines.
