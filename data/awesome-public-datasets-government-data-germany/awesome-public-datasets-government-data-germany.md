# Awesome Public Datasets – Government Data: Germany

**Source:** [GENESIS-Online – Statistisches Bundesamt (Destatis)](https://www-genesis.destatis.de/genesis/online)

Part of the Awesome Public Datasets (APD) collection, this entry points to GENESIS-Online, the official database of the German Federal Statistical Office, offering structured access to detailed official statistics for Germany.

---

## Features

- **Official statistics database**: Contains results from Germany’s official (amtliche) statistics.
- **Deeply structured data**: Data are "tief gegliederte Ergebnisse", meaning finely disaggregated and highly structured statistical tables.
- **Continuously expanded**: The database is regularly and continuously extended with new and updated statistical results.
- **Free table access**: Retrieval of statistical tables (Tabellenabruf) is free of charge.
- **Customizable queries**: Table queries can be flexibly adjusted to individual user needs (e.g., selecting variables, breakdowns, and time ranges).
- **Multiple export formats**: Results can be saved in various file formats, enabling reuse and further analysis.
