## Awesome AI in Finance

**URL:** https://github.com/georgezouq/awesome-ai-in-finance#readme  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** awesome-lists, AI, financial

### Overview
Awesome AI in Finance is a curated, open-source directory of research, tools, libraries, and educational resources focused on applying artificial intelligence and machine learning to financial markets. It aggregates material for tasks such as automated trading, quantitative research, portfolio management, and financial analysis.

A Chinese-language companion list is also available.

### Main Sections
- **Agents** – AI and multi-agent trading platforms and frameworks.
- **LLMs** – Benchmarks, frameworks, strategies, and examples of using large language models in finance and trading.
- **Papers** – Foundational and modern academic research (e.g., early work like Bachelier’s *The Theory of Speculation*).
- **Courses & Books** – Educational content on AI, ML, and quant techniques in finance.
- **Strategies & Research**
  - Time Series Data
  - Portfolio Management
  - High Frequency Trading
  - Event-Driven Strategies
  - Crypto Currencies Strategies
  - Technical Analysis
  - Lottery & Gambling
  - Arbitrage
- **Data Sources** – Datasets and APIs for financial data.
- **Research Tools** – Libraries, toolkits, and environments for quantitative and ML research.
- **Trading System** – Frameworks and infrastructure for building trading systems.
- **TA-Lib** – Resources and examples around the TA-Lib technical analysis library.
- **Exchange API** – APIs and SDKs for interacting with exchanges.
- **Articles** – Articles and posts on AI in finance.
- **Others** – Miscellaneous related resources.

### Notable Entries
**Agents**
- **nofx** – Multi-exchange AI trading platform with multi-AI competition, self-evolution, and real-time dashboard.
- **TradingAgents** – Multi-agent LLM financial trading framework.
- **FinRobot** – Open-source AI agent platform for financial analysis using LLMs.

**LLMs**
- **Nof1** – Benchmark that measures AI investing ability by allocating real money to models in live markets with identical prompts and data.
- **AI Hedge Fund** – Repository exploring AI-driven trading decisions.
- **MarS** – Financial market simulation engine powered by generative foundation models.
- **Financial Statement Analysis with LLMs** – Study showing GPT-4’s performance on earnings prediction, narrative insight generation, and trading strategies.
- **PIXIU** – Open-source financial LLM, 136K-instruction dataset, and evaluation benchmark.
- **FinGPT** – Playground for LLMs and NLP in finance.
- **Indicator & Strategy Examples**
  - MACD + RSI + ADX Strategy (ChatGPT-powered) on TradingView.
  - Discussion of a ChatGPT-based trading algorithm that achieved high returns.
  - Example of using ChatGPT to adjust strategy parameters.
  - Hands-on LLMs project for building a real-time financial advisor chatbot.
  - ChatGPT-based crypto selection strategy using technical indicators.
