# Awesome Artificial Intelligence

**URL:** https://github.com/owainlewis/awesome-artificial-intelligence#readme

**Publisher / Brand:** owainlewis

## Overview
Awesome Artificial Intelligence is a curated, GitHub-hosted directory of actively maintained resources for building and shipping AI systems. It emphasizes AI engineering topics such as retrieval‑augmented generation (RAG), agents, evaluation, guardrails, and deployment, alongside selected books, guides, papers, courses, and tools.

## Features

### Core Focus
- Curated collection of “must‑use” and actively maintained AI resources
- Emphasis on **AI engineering**:
  - Retrieval‑Augmented Generation (RAG)
  - Agents
  - Evals (evaluation of AI systems)
  - Guardrails and safety mechanisms
  - Deployment and productionization
- Mix of conceptual learning resources and practical tooling

### Core Resources (Evergreen)
Resources intended to remain valuable over time regardless of changing tools.

#### Books
- **Modern & Practical** AI books (focused on current methods and applied AI)
- **Foundational** AI books (covering core theory and historical foundations)

#### AI Engineering
Frameworks, patterns, and references for building robust AI systems.

- **Guides & Playbooks**
  - Written guidance on AI system design and implementation
  - Practical playbooks for moving from prototypes to production

- **Frameworks**
  - Libraries and frameworks to structure AI applications and workflows

- **Retrieval‑Augmented Generation (RAG)**
  - Resources on architectures, patterns, and tooling for RAG systems

- **Evals**
  - Tools and methodologies for evaluating AI models and applications

#### Landmark Papers
- Influential research papers that shaped modern AI
- Focus on explaining the rationale and architectures underlying contemporary methods

### Learning Resources

#### Courses
Structured educational material across levels:
- **Beginner** courses for entry‑level understanding
- **Intermediate / Advanced** courses for deeper or specialized study
- **Focused** courses targeting particular subdomains or skills

#### Newsletters
- Curated list of AI newsletters
- Helps users stay current on AI developments while reducing information overload

### Tools
Catalog of tools for building and deploying AI applications.

- **Models**
  - Links to or references for AI/LLM models and model providers

- **Code & Developer Tools**
  - Developer‑oriented utilities for integrating, testing, and managing AI in software

- **Multimedia AI Tools**
  - **Image**: tools for image generation, editing, or analysis
  - **Video**: tools for video generation, editing, or understanding
  - **Audio**: tools for audio generation, transcription, or analysis

### Repository Structure & Metadata
- Public GitHub repository (`owainlewis/awesome-artificial-intelligence`)
- Versioning and history via commits
- Standard open‑source metadata files (e.g., `LICENSE`, `pyproject.toml`)
- Archived subdirectory for older or superseded resources
