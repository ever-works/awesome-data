## Overview

Awesome Legal NLP is a comprehensive collection of natural language processing resources specifically focused on legal text analysis, including datasets, pre-trained models, research papers, and tools for processing legal documents.

## Key Categories

- **Legal Datasets** - Annotated corpora and legal text collections
- **Pre-trained Models** - NLP models fine-tuned for legal text
- **Tools & Libraries** - Software for legal text processing
- **Research Papers** - Academic publications on legal NLP
- **Case Law Analysis** - Tools for analyzing judicial decisions
- **Contract Analysis** - NLP tools for contract review

## Featured Resources

- BERT and transformer models for legal text
- Legal entity recognition tools
- Contract clause extraction systems
- Legal document classification models
- Citation analysis tools
- Legal question answering systems

## Applications

- Automated contract review
- Legal document summarization
- Case law prediction
- Legal information extraction
- Regulatory compliance checking
- Legal citation analysis

## Research Focus

Maintained by Maastricht Law & Tech Lab, focusing on cutting-edge research in legal natural language processing and computational law.
