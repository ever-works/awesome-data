## Overview

r/RoastMyStartup is a Reddit community dedicated to providing brutally honest, no-holds-barred feedback on startup ideas, products, websites, and business strategies.

## Community Philosophy

- **Brutal Honesty**: Community members don't sugarcoat their feedback
- **Assumption Testing**: Designed to challenge your core business assumptions
- **Blind Spot Discovery**: Find problems you didn't know existed
- **Reality Check**: Get perspectives that friends and family won't provide
- **Constructive Criticism**: Harsh but aimed at helping you succeed

## What to Expect

- Direct, unfiltered criticism of your idea or product
- Questions that challenge your business model
- UX/UI critiques of your website or app
- Market fit analysis and positioning feedback
- Pricing strategy evaluation
- Competitive analysis from community members

## Best Use Cases

- **Pre-Launch Validation**: Test your idea before investing heavily
- **Website Critique**: Get feedback on landing pages and user flows
- **Positioning Review**: Ensure your messaging resonates
- **Feature Prioritization**: Learn what users actually care about
- **Reality Check**: Confirm or challenge your assumptions

## How to Get the Most Value

1. **Come with Thick Skin**: Expect harsh feedback
2. **Be Specific**: Ask targeted questions about specific aspects
3. **Provide Context**: Share your goals and target audience
4. **Stay Open-Minded**: Don't get defensive about criticism
5. **Follow Up**: Engage with commenters to dig deeper

## What Gets Roasted

- Unclear value propositions
- Poor website design or user experience
- Unrealistic business models
- Overpriced products
- Solutions looking for problems
- Bad naming or branding choices
- Weak competitive positioning

## Benefits

- Free, honest feedback from real users
- Identify problems early before they're expensive to fix
- Test messaging and positioning
- Validate or invalidate assumptions
- Build a thicker skin for entrepreneurship
- Sometimes find early supporters despite the roasting

## Important Note

This is not a supportive, cheerleading community. Come prepared for harsh criticism, but remember that it's intended to help you build a better product.

## Target Audience

Founders who want honest feedback over validation, especially those in early stages who can still pivot based on criticism.
