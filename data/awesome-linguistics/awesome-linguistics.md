# Awesome Linguistics

**URL:** https://github.com/theimpossibleastronaut/awesome-linguistics#readme  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** awesome-lists, linguistics, research

## Summary
Awesome Linguistics is an Awesome-style, alphabetically organized, curated directory of resources, tools, and references related to linguistics and language science. It aggregates programming tools, datasets, models, books, and community links relevant to both research and practical language technology work.

## Features

- **Curated Awesome-style list**
  - Focused on linguistics and language science
  - Follows the Awesome list conventions and formatting
  - Alphabetically sorted structure for easier browsing

- **Programming-related resources**
  - Libraries and frameworks for linguistic analysis and NLP
  - Applications and tools useful for developing language-related software

- **Platforms and toolkits**
  - Collections of platforms and toolkits oriented toward linguistic research and language technology

- **Algorithms**
  - References to algorithms relevant to linguistics and natural language processing

- **Datasets**
  - Linguistic and language corpora
  - Datasets for training and evaluating language models and linguistic tools

- **General resources**
  - Guides, articles, and reference materials on linguistics
  - Educational and research-focused links

- **Deep learning models and transformers**
  - Collections of neural models and transformer-based systems for language tasks

- **Reference sections by medium**
  - **On Wikipedia:** Key linguistics topics and concept pages
  - **On YouTube:** Channels, lectures, and video series related to linguistics

- **Books**
  - Highlighted as “more interesting and complete” works
  - **Free books:** Open-access and no-cost textbooks or references
  - **Non-free books:** Paid or commercially published works

- **Standards**
  - Linguistic and language technology standards (formats, protocols, etc.)

- **Lists**
  - Meta-lists and related curated collections around linguistics and language tech

- **Communities**
  - Online communities, forums, and groups for linguistics and language science

- **Project metadata and governance**
  - LICENSE file specifying usage and redistribution rights
  - CONTRIBUTING guidelines for adding or updating resources
  - Uses GitHub workflows configuration (automation/CI related to the list)
