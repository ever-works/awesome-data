## Overview

The HUD USER data portal provides public access to datasets produced or curated by HUD’s Office of Policy Development and Research (PD&R). These datasets focus on U.S. housing, community development, and related policy research, and are intended for analysts, researchers, policymakers, and practitioners working with housing and urban development data.
