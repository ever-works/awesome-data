### Overview
Meta Awesome - PaaS is an "awesome-style" meta list that aggregates curated Awesome directories and resource lists focused on Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) technologies. Instead of listing tools directly, it collects and organizes links to other high-quality Awesome lists and curated resources in the PaaS domain.

### Features
- **Meta aggregation of PaaS resources**  
  Collects links to multiple "Awesome" lists and curated directories related to PaaS.

- **Focus on PaaS technologies**  
  Concentrates specifically on Platform-as-a-Service platforms, tools, and ecosystems in the cloud space.

- **Awesome-style structure**  
  Follows the common Awesome list conventions (Markdown list format, curated links, community-driven contributions).

- **Central entry point for PaaS lists**  
  Serves as a single reference point to discover other PaaS-related Awesome lists and curated collections.

- **Open and community-maintained (via GitHub)**  
  Hosted on GitHub, allowing users to submit pull requests and issues to expand or improve the meta list (in line with standard Awesome/GitHub workflows).
