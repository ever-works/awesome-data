## Overview

Hacker News is the premier online community for startup founders, developers, and technology professionals, run by Y Combinator.

## Show HN

- **Launch Format**: "Show HN:" prefix
- **Product Demos**: Share what you've built
- **Community Feedback**: Engage with commenters
- **Viral Potential**: Front page = massive traffic
- **Strict Guidelines**: Follow HN culture

- Community engagement
