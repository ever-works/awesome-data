## Overview

Awesome Robotics Datasets provides a curated collection of datasets for robotics and computer vision research and development.

## Featured Datasets

- **UZH-FPV Drone Racing Dataset** - First-person view drone racing data
- **The Blackbird Dataset** - Aggressive autonomous flight dataset
- **KITTI Dataset** - Autonomous driving benchmark
- **TUM RGB-D** - RGB-D SLAM benchmark
- **EuRoC MAV** - Micro aerial vehicle dataset

## Dataset Categories

- **SLAM & Localization**
  - Visual SLAM datasets
  - LiDAR SLAM datasets
  - Multi-sensor datasets

- **Autonomous Driving**
  - Urban driving scenarios
  - Highway datasets
  - Off-road environments

- **Aerial Robotics**
  - Drone racing datasets
  - Aerial mapping data
  - MAV datasets

- **Manipulation**
  - Grasping datasets
  - Object manipulation
  - Warehouse scenarios

## Data Types
