## Overview
Meta Awesome - PHP Magazines is a curated, Awesome-style meta-directory that aggregates lists and directories of PHP-related magazines and periodicals. It serves as a central reference point to discover resources focused on PHP news, articles, and ongoing publication series.

## Features
- **Meta-directory of PHP magazines**: Aggregates links to various magazines and periodicals specifically focused on PHP.
- **Awesome-style organization**: Follows the familiar "Awesome list" convention for structure and curation.
- **Curated external lists**: Points to other curated lists or directories, rather than attempting to catalog every individual article.
- **Focus on PHP ecosystem**: Concentrates on publications relevant to PHP development, tools, frameworks, and community news.
- **Git-based collaboration**: Hosted on GitHub, allowing community contributions via pull requests and issues (standard GitHub project workflow).

## Pricing
- Not a commercial product or service – it is an open, GitHub-hosted curated document with no pricing or paid plans.
