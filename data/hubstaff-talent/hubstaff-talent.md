## Overview

Hubstaff Talent is a completely free platform for remote work, connecting freelancers and full-time remote workers with companies without any fees.

## Key Features

- 100% free (no fees ever)
- Freelance and full-time
- Global talent pool
- No commissions
- Direct connections

## Categories

- Technology and development
- Creative and design
- Customer support
- Freelance projects
- Full-time remote roles

## Unique Selling Point

- No fees for workers
- No fees for employers
- Simple platform
- Direct hiring

Part of the established free remote job platform ecosystem.
