# Awesome Suricata

**Description**  
Curated, community-maintained list of resources related to Suricata, an open‑source intrusion detection/prevention system (IDS/IPS) and network security monitoring engine.

## Features

- **Curated resource collection** focused specifically on Suricata and its ecosystem.
- **Organized contents** with sections including (as listed in the README):
  - **Input Tools** – tools and utilities for feeding data/traffic/logs into Suricata.
  - **Output Tools** – tools that consume or transform Suricata’s output formats (alerts, logs, events).
  - **Operations, Monitoring and Troubleshooting** – resources for running, monitoring, and diagnosing Suricata in production.
  - **Programming Libraries and Toolkits** – libraries and SDKs to integrate Suricata data or functionality into other software.
  - **Dashboards and Templates** – pre-built dashboards, visualizations, and templates for Suricata metrics and alerts.
  - **Development Tools** – tools that support Suricata development, testing, or rule authoring.
  - **Documentation and Guides** – tutorials, reference docs, and how‑to guides about Suricata.
  - **Analysis Tools** – utilities for investigating, correlating, or post‑processing Suricata data.
  - **Rule Sets** – collections of detection rules tailored for Suricata.
  - **Rule/Security Content Management and Handling** – tools for managing, updating, or organizing rule content.
  - **Plugins and Extensions** – add‑ons that extend Suricata functionality.
- **Community contributions** guided by a CONTRIBUTING file.
- **Code of conduct** included for community participation.
- **Actively maintained GitHub repository** with version history, stars, and forks visible for transparency.
