## Overview

Kubernetes Failure Stories compiles public failure and horror stories related to Kubernetes to help SRE, Ops, and platform/infrastructure teams learn from others and reduce unknown unknowns of running Kubernetes in production.

## Features

- **Real Production Incidents**: Stories from major companies including Datadog, Spotify, and Moonlight
- **Detailed Postmortems**: Analysis of what went wrong and lessons learned
- **Diverse Failure Modes**: Covers etcd, CoreDNS, AWS, GKE, and other Kubernetes components
- **Conference Talks**: Links to presentations like Spotify's "How Spotify Accidentally Deleted All its Kube Clusters with No User Impact"
- **Community Contributions**: Open for submissions of public failure stories

## Notable Examples

- Datadog's "10 Ways to Shoot Yourself in the Foot with Kubernetes"
- Moonlight's "All pods scheduled to same failing host" with 100% traffic loss
- Various AWS and GKE production incident reports

## Purpose

Makes it easier for Kubernetes operators to learn from real-world failures, understand potential failure modes, and implement better practices in their own deployments.
