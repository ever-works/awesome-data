# Awesome Boilerplate Projects

**Website:** https://github.com/melvin0008/awesome-projects-boilerplates#readme

## Overview
Awesome Boilerplate Projects is a curated, open-source list of boilerplates, templates, and starter kits for quickly bootstrapping mobile, web, and backend applications across multiple languages and frameworks.

## Features
- **Curated boilerplates and templates** focused on productivity and quick project setup.
- **Coverage across major platforms and stacks:**
  - **Android Development** – boilerplates and templates for Android apps.
  - **iOS Development** – boilerplates and templates for iOS apps.
  - **JavaScript** – boilerplates and generators for modern web applications, including:
    - [Yeoman](https://github.com/yeoman): scaffolding tool with many generators for web apps.
    - [Mega Boilerplate](https://github.com/sahat/megaboilerplate): full‑stack boilerplate generator (Node.js, static sites, Electron apps).
    - Subsections for popular JS libraries/frameworks:
      - Node.js
      - AngularJS
      - ReactJS
      - HyperApp
      - Backbone
      - jQuery
      - Polymer
      - Electron
  - **Hybrid Mobile App Frameworks** – boilerplates for cross‑platform mobile apps.
  - **Microservices** – templates for microservice-based architectures.
  - **Python** – boilerplates and templates for Python applications.
  - **PHP** – boilerplates and templates for PHP projects.
  - **Ruby** – boilerplates and templates for Ruby applications.
  - **.NET** – boilerplates and templates for C#.NET and ASP.NET projects.
  - **Go** – boilerplates and templates for Go applications.
  - **Browser Webextensions** – templates for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and other browser extensions.
  - **Websites** – generic website boilerplates and templates.
  - **IDE templates** – templating systems and starter configurations for different IDEs.
  - **GitHub boilerplates** – template files and boilerplates for use on GitHub.
- **Open contribution model** – community contributions encouraged via a CONTRIBUTING guide.
- **Clear structure** via categorized sections to help developers quickly find relevant starters.
