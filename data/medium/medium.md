## Overview

Medium is a popular publishing platform where writers share long-form content, build audiences, and can earn money through the Partner Program.

## Key Features

- Clean reading experience
- Built-in audience
- Publications and topics
- Partner Program earnings
- SEO benefits
- Social features

## Partner Program

- Get paid for content
- Based on reading time
- Member-only content
- Revenue sharing

## Publications

- Join established publications
- Build distribution
- Reach larger audiences
- Editorial support
- Topic specialization

## Content Types

- Technical tutorials
- Startup stories
- Product insights
- Industry analysis
- Personal essays
- How-to guides

## Benefits

- Large built-in audience
- Easy to publish
- No hosting needed
- SEO advantages
- Earnings potential

## Considerations

- Paywall for some content
- Platform dependency
- Algorithm changes
- Membership required for full access

## Target Audience

Writers, founders, and professionals building personal brands through content.

## Pricing

- Free to publish
- $5/month membership to read all content
- Partner Program available
