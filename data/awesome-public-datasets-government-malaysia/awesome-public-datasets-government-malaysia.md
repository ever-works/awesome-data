# Awesome Public Datasets – Government: Malaysia

**Official Name:** data.gov.my  
**Type:** Government open data portal (Malaysia)  
**Source:** https://data.gov.my/

## Overview

The Government of Malaysia’s official open data portal provides high-frequency, high-granularity datasets and dashboards across multiple public sectors. It targets general citizens, researchers, and application developers, offering both downloadable datasets and programmatic access via APIs.

## Features

- **Central open data catalogue**  
  - One-stop portal to browse Malaysia’s public sector open data.  
  - Data catalogue with search to find specific datasets.  
  - Supports both file download and programmatic access (APIs) where available.

- **Dashboards (data visualisation)**  
  - Curated dashboard collection showcasing key government data.  
  - Highlighted “hottest” / most-viewed dashboards, including examples such as:  
    - **JPJ – Car Popularity**  
    - **JPJ – Vehicle Registrations**  
    - **Prasarana – Rapid Explorer**  
    - **JPN – Birthday Popularity**  
    - **BNM – Interest Rates**

- **Popular datasets**  
  - Public listing of most accessed datasets, with indicators like view counts.  
  - Example featured datasets:  
    - DOSM – Population Table: States  
    - DOSM – Population Table: Administrative Districts  
    - DOSM – Population Table: Malaysia  
    - MOF – Price of Petroleum & Diesel  
    - JPJ – Vehicle Registration Transactions: Cars

- **Target users and use cases**  
  - Regular citizens seeking government information.  
  - Researchers and analysts needing structured data tables.  
  - Application developers integrating government data via APIs.

- **Data characteristics**  
  - Emphasis on:  
    - High frequency (frequent updates).  
    - High granularity (detailed, disaggregated data).  
    - High impact (datasets suitable for analysis, apps, and policy-related work).

- **Governance and ownership**  
  - Officially provided by the **Government of Malaysia**.  
  - Part of the **Public Sector Open Data** initiative.  
  - Marked as **Open Data** and **Open Source** in the site footer.

- **Usage information**  
  - Basic usage/traffic indicators over time (e.g., for years like 2023–2025), showing how the portal is being used.

## Pricing

- Access to the portal and its datasets is presented as **open data**, implying free public access.  
- No paid plans or pricing tiers are mentioned in the provided content.
