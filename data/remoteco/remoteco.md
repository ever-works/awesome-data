## Overview

Remote.co is a comprehensive remote work platform that combines job listings with extensive editorial content, resources, and guides for remote workers and companies.

## Key Features

- Job listings across industries
- Company profiles and reviews
- Remote work guides and articles
- Industry insights
- Remote work best practices
- Company culture information

## Strengths

- **Volume**: Large number of listings
- **Industry Variety**: Broad range of sectors
- **Rich Content**: Educational resources
- **Company Insights**: Detailed profiles
- **Free Access**: No cost for job seekers

## Comparison

### vs We Work Remotely
- More content vs simpler job board
- Industry variety vs tech focus
- Resource hub vs direct listings

### vs FlexJobs
- Free vs paid
- More volume vs hand-vetted
- Less screening vs quality focus

## Target Audience

Remote workers across all industries seeking jobs and learning about remote work culture.

## Pricing

Free for job seekers.
