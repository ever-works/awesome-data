## Overview
This entry in the Awesome Public Datasets list points to the City of Gatineau’s official open data portal. The portal provides structured access to municipal datasets for Gatineau (population ~298,000), located on the north shore of the Ottawa River in Quebec, Canada.

## Features
- **Municipal Open Data Portal**  
  Central access point to datasets published by the Ville de Gatineau.

- **Open Data Principles (Sunlight Foundation)**  
  Gatineau explicitly endorses the ten core principles of open data as defined by the Sunlight Foundation. Data published through the portal is intended to be:
  - Complète (complete)
  - Primaire (primary)
  - Opportune (timely)
  - Accessible
  - Exploitable (machine-processable)
  - Non discriminatoire (available to anyone)
  - Non propriétaire (non-proprietary formats)
  - Libres de droits (license-free / open license)
  - Permanente (permanently available)
  - À moindre coût (low cost / ideally free)

- **Government Transparency Orientation**  
  Alignment with international open-data best practices to promote reuse and analysis of civic information.

- **Municipal Context**  
  Data pertains to a mid-sized Canadian city (Gatineau), enabling local, regional, and comparative urban analyses.

## Use Cases
