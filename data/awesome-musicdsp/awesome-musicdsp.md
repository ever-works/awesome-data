## Overview

Awesome MusicDSP is a carefully curated collection of the best music DSP and audio programming resources for developers and researchers.

## Features

- **Code Libraries**: Production-ready DSP libraries and frameworks
- **DSP Cookbooks**: Practical algorithms and implementations
- **DSP Playgrounds**: Interactive environments for learning and experimentation
- **Free Online Textbooks**: Comprehensive digital signal processing textbooks
- **Online Courses**: Structured learning paths for audio DSP
- **Machine Learning**: AI/ML applications in audio processing
- **Technical Reading**: Research papers, articles, and documentation
- **Audio Frameworks**: JUCE, iPlug2, and other professional frameworks
- **Programming Languages**: Resources for Faust (Functional Audio Stream) and other audio languages

## Topics Covered

- Digital filters and equalizers
- Audio effects (reverb, delay, distortion)
- Synthesis techniques
- Spectral analysis
- Time-stretching and pitch-shifting
- Physical modeling
- Room acoustics simulation

## Use Cases

- Audio plugin development
- Music software creation
- Game audio engines
- Research in audio processing
- Educational projects
- Professional audio tools
