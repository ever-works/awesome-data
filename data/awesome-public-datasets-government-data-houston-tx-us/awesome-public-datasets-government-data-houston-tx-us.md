# Awesome Public Datasets – Government Data: Houston, TX, US

**Website:** http://data.houstontx.gov/  
**Provider / Brand:** City of Houston  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** government, datasets, open-data

## Overview
The City of Houston Open Data portal provides public access to a wide range of municipal datasets. It is intended to support transparency, accountability, civic engagement, and data-driven innovation within Houston.

## Features
- **Central Open Data Portal**
  - Single access point for the City of Houston’s municipal datasets.
  - Web-based interface for browsing, viewing, and downloading data.

- **Search Functionality**
  - Search bar to find datasets by keyword.
  - Ability to discover datasets across departments and topics.

- **Popular Tags**
  - Tag-based navigation to quickly locate datasets by commonly used topics.

- **Dataset Grouping**
  - **Groups section** to browse datasets organized into thematic or departmental groups.
  - Visual group icons (e.g., public safety, environmental data, infrastructure, etc. as implied by icons like bars, care, planet-earth, rainfall-geographic-distribution).

- **Showcases (Thematic Views / Applications)**
  - **MyCity** – a showcase highlighting selected datasets or a city-centric data experience.
  - **Open Finance** – focus on financial and budget-related transparency datasets.
  - **Texas Public Information Act** – resources/datasets related to public information requests and compliance.
  - **Police Transparency Hub** – curated access to public safety and police-related transparency data.

- **Popular Datasets Listing**
  - Dedicated section showing frequently accessed datasets, including:
    - **Payroll**
    - **Houston Police Department Crime Statistics**
    - **City of Houston Building Code Enforcement Violations (DON)**
    - **Archived Solicitations**
    - **Listing of all City of Houston Vendors/Suppliers**

- **New and Recent Datasets**
  - Section to highlight recently added or updated datasets, including:
    - **City of Houston Median Gross Rent**
    - **City of Houston Median Household Income and Median Housing Value**
    - **Payroll** (recent version/refresh)
    - **City Dates**
    - **City of Houston Residential Building Permits by Month and Year**

- **Policy and Governance Information**
  - Links to:
    - **FAQs** (general City of Houston questions)
    - **Privacy Policy**
    - **Terms of Use** for the data portal

- **Contact Channel**
  - **Contact Us** page for inquiries related to the open data portal or datasets.

- **Technical Platform**
  - Portal branding indicates use of **OpenGov** as the underlying platform/technology for hosting and managing the data.

## Data Types and Topics (Examples)
While the portal includes many datasets, the content highlights examples in areas such as:
- **Public Safety** – crime statistics, police transparency.
- **Human Resources / Administration** – city payroll.
- **Code Enforcement & Permitting** – building code violations, residential building permits.
- **Procurement & Vendors** – archived solicitations, vendor/supplier listings.
- **Housing & Economic Indicators** – median gross rent, median household income, median housing value.
- **Civic Information** – city dates and events.

## Pricing
Access to the City of Houston Open Data portal and its public datasets is presented as open and publicly available. No pricing or paid plans are indicated in the provided content.
