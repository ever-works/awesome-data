## awesome-digital-wellbeing

**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** health, lifestyle, productivity  
**Website:** https://github.com/jcanfield/awesome-digital-wellbeing

### Overview
awesome-digital-wellbeing is an open-source, “awesome list”-style curated directory of resources focused on digital wellbeing. It helps people reduce unhealthy screen time, build healthier digital habits, and live more offline in a highly connected world.

### Features
- **Curated resource list:** Centralized collection of links, tools, articles, and projects related to digital wellbeing and healthier technology use.
- **Awesome-list format:** Follows the common “awesome” convention, making it easy to scan, browse, and contribute.
- **Offline-focused perspective:** Emphasis on helping users live more intentionally offline despite being in an online world.
- **Open contribution model:** Public `contributing.md` with guidelines for adding or improving resources.
- **Community standards:** `code-of-conduct.md` to define expected behavior for contributors.
- **Clear licensing:** `LICENSE.md` included to specify reuse and contribution terms.
- **Static index page:** `index.html` provides a web-friendly view of the curated list directly from the repository.
- **Actively maintained repository:** Version and last-commit badges indicate ongoing updates and curation.

- Free, open-source resource directory (no paid plans mentioned).
