## Overview

Awesome AI Market Maps is a comprehensive collection of over 400 AI market maps from 2026, 2025, and 2024. These maps provide visual representations of the AI landscape, helping professionals understand the competitive environment, identify trends, and discover emerging players across various AI sectors.

## Features

- **400+ Market Maps**: Extensive collection of AI landscape visualizations
- **Multiple Sources**: Maps from top VCs, industry analysts, and AI practitioners
- **Recent Coverage**: Includes maps from 2024, 2025, and 2026
- **Sector Analysis**: Coverage of various AI sectors including:
  - Generative AI
  - MLOps and AI Infrastructure
  - AI Applications
  - Enterprise AI
  - Consumer AI
  - AI Security
  - AI Agents
  - Computer Vision
  - Natural Language Processing
- **Investment Insights**: Valuable resource for investors tracking AI market trends
- **Strategic Planning**: Helps companies identify competitors and partnership opportunities
- **Regularly Updated**: New maps added as they become available

## Use Cases

- Market research and competitive analysis
- Investment decision-making
- Strategic partnership identification
- Trend analysis and forecasting
- Educational resource for understanding AI ecosystem
