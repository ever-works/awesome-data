Awesome Weekly Robotics showcases projects and innovations featured in the Weekly Robotics newsletter, providing curated robotics content.

## Drone Autopilot Software

- **ArduPilot** - Open source autopilot for multirotors, fixed wings, rovers, boats, and submarines
- **PX4** - Open source flight control software for drones and other unmanned vehicles
- **Betaflight** - Flight controller firmware for racing drones
- **iNav** - Navigation-focused flight controller software
