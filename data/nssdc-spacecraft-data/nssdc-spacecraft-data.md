# NSSDC Spacecraft Data

**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** datasets, space, physics  
**Brand:** NASA

## Overview
NSSDC Spacecraft Data is a NASA National Space Science Data Center archive that provides access to data from hundreds of spacecraft missions. It is presented as part of an "awesome-style" curated list of physics and space mission datasets, aimed at researchers, educators, and enthusiasts who need mission and instrument data from across the history of spaceflight.

## Features
- **Mission Archive Coverage**
  - Data from hundreds of spacecraft missions.  
  - Focus on space science and physics-related missions.

- **Spacecraft & Instrument Data**
  - Spacecraft-level datasets (e.g., mission operations, trajectories, key measurements).  
  - Physics and space environment measurements from various instruments.

- **National Space Science Data Center (NSSDC) Access Point**
  - Official NASA archival resource managed by NSSDC.  
  - Long-term preservation and curation of space science data.

- **Research-Oriented Dataset Collection**
  - Suitable for scientific analysis in space physics, astrophysics, planetary science, and related fields.  
  - Can be used for mission comparison, historical studies, and educational projects.

- **Inclusion in Curated Dataset Lists**
  - Listed within an "awesome-style" directory of physics and space datasets, making it easier to discover alongside related data resources.

- **Web-Based Access**
  - Accessed via the NSSDC data request/obtaining-data page.  
  - Publicly reachable through the NSSDC website.
