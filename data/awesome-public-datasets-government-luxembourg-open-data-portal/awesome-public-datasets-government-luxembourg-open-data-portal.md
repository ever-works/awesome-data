## Awesome Public Datasets – Government: Luxembourg Open Data Portal

**URL:** https://data.public.lu/en/

### Overview
The Luxembourg Open Data Portal is the national platform for public sector open data in Luxembourg. It aggregates datasets, files, and related reuses from a wide range of Luxembourgish public organizations and makes them freely accessible for analysis, applications, and research.

Key current stats highlighted on the portal:
- **2,407+ datasets**
- **88,805+ files**
- **176+ reuses** (applications, analyses, or services built on the data)
- **2,531+ users**
- **206+ organizations**
- **593+ discussions**

### Features

#### Data Access & Discovery
- Centralized **search interface** for:
  - Datasets
  - Reuses
  - Organizations
- Ability to **filter and browse** datasets (including featured, latest, and reference datasets).
- Access to **individual dataset pages** with detailed metadata and download links.
- Example of available data: official **orthophotos of Luxembourg** (e.g., winter 2025 edition) from the Administration du cadastre et de la topographie.

#### Thematic Topics
Curated topic pages that group related datasets, including:
- **Geospatial data**
- **Earth observation and environment**
- **Meteorological data**
- **Statistics**
- **Mobility**
- **Companies**
- Additional thematic collections under **“All featured topics”**.

#### Community & Reuse
- **Reuses** section listing projects, apps, or analyses built using portal datasets.
- **Organizations** directory of public bodies publishing data.
- **User accounts** (log in / sign up) to participate more actively.
- **Discussions** area (593+ discussions) for interaction around data use and issues.

#### Publication & Contribution
- **Contribute** section with options to:
  - Publish a dataset
  - Publish a reuse
- Online forms for authorized publishers to upload data and document reuses.

#### Language & Accessibility
- **Bilingual interface**: French (FR) and English (EN).
- Separate language URLs for FR/EN versions of the portal.
- Basic fallback information for users with JavaScript disabled (degraded experience notice).

#### News & Updates
- **News section** highlighting new or enriched datasets and initiatives, for example:
  - Publication of enriched **eHealth indicators** in open format, providing monthly key indicators on the use of eHealth services in Luxembourg.
- **All news** archive page for browsing past announcements.

### Pricing
The portal is a **government open data platform**. The content indicates free public access to datasets; no pricing or paid plans are mentioned.
