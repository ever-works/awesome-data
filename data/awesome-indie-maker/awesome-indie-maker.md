## Overview

Awesome Indie Maker provides a structured collection of resources organized around the maker journey: Idea, Build, Launch, Grow, Monetize, and Automate.

## Features

- Idea validation tools and market research resources
- No-code and low-code development platforms
- Design tools and UI kits for rapid prototyping
- Launch strategies and Product Hunt guidelines
- Growth hacking techniques and SEO resources
- Email marketing and newsletter platforms
- Payment processing and subscription management
- Analytics and metrics tracking tools
- Automation tools to reduce manual work
- Time management and productivity systems

## Pricing

Free and open-source.
