## Overview

Awesome GNN for Time Series (GNN4TS) focuses on applying graph neural networks to time series problems, particularly spatial-temporal forecasting and analysis.

## Spatial-Temporal Data

Many real-world systems have both spatial and temporal dimensions:
- Traffic networks
- Sensor networks
- Climate data
- Social networks
- Financial markets

## Key Applications

### Traffic Forecasting
Predicting traffic flow, speed, or demand across road networks using graph structures.

### Weather Prediction
Modeling relationships between weather stations and atmospheric dynamics.

### Energy Systems
Forecasting power consumption and generation across grids.

### Epidemic Modeling
Predicting disease spread across geographical regions.

## Model Architectures

- Spatio-temporal graph convolution
- Temporal graph networks
- Attention-based methods
- Recurrent GNN variants

## Datasets

Common benchmarks:
- METR-LA, PEMS-BAY (traffic)
- NYC Taxi/Bike
- COVID-19 spread
- Climate data
