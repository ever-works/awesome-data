# Iranis

**Category:** Datasets  
**Tags:** datasets, computer-vision, transportation

A large-scale dataset of Farsi license plate characters (numbers, letters, and symbols) extracted from real-world license plate images captured by various cameras. The dataset is designed for character recognition and license plate recognition research.

## Features

- **Scale and content**
  - More than **83,000** labeled character images (total **83,844** instances)
  - Characters cropped from real-world Farsi license plates
  - Includes numbers, letters, and a special symbol category
