## Overview

Open Sustainable Technology is a curated directory of open source projects that preserve, analyze, or support natural ecosystems through technology, covering climate modeling, renewable energy optimization, biodiversity tracking, and environmental monitoring.

## Features

- Climate modeling and weather prediction tools
- Renewable energy system design and optimization software
- Environmental monitoring and sensor networks
- Biodiversity tracking and conservation tools
- Water resource management systems
- Land use and deforestation monitoring
- Carbon accounting and emissions tracking
- Sustainable agriculture and precision farming tools
- Interactive database at docs.getgrist.com/gSscJkc5Rb1R/OpenSustaintech
- Community contributions and collaborative development
