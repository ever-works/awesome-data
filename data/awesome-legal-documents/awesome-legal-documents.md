## Overview

Awesome Legal Documents provides a curated list of free legal document templates and resources that companies can use for their business operations, covering everything from open source licenses to privacy policies and employment agreements.

## Key Categories

- **Open Source Licenses** - Various OSI-approved licenses for software projects
- **Privacy Policies** - Templates for GDPR, CCPA, and general privacy compliance
- **Terms of Service** - User agreement templates for websites and applications
- **Employment Documents** - Offer letters, NDAs, and contractor agreements
- **Business Agreements** - Vendor agreements, partnership documents
- **Intellectual Property** - Copyright notices, patent disclosures

## Featured Resources

- MIT, Apache, GPL and other open source licenses
- GDPR-compliant privacy policy templates
- Terms of service for SaaS applications
- Employee agreement templates
- Contributor license agreements (CLAs)
- Cookie policy templates

## Benefits

- Free and openly available documents
- Community-reviewed templates
- Save legal costs for startups
- Standardized legal language
- Easy to customize for your needs

## Important Note

While these templates are useful starting points, companies should consult with qualified legal professionals to ensure documents meet their specific needs and comply with applicable laws.
