## Overview

LaunchingNext is an established startup directory that has been around for years, now featuring thousands of startups, side projects, and innovative business ideas.

## Features

- **Free Submissions**: Submit your startup at no cost
- **Dofollow Backlinks**: DR 50 domain rating for SEO benefits
- **Newsletter Features**: Selected listings get featured in the newsletter
- **Wide Visibility**: Thousands of existing listings provide context and discovery
- **Long-term Discovery**: Products remain searchable over time
