## Overview

Awesome FinOps Resources is a curated collection of FinOps (Financial Operations) materials that help organizations optimize cloud spending and make data-driven decisions about cloud investments. This repository is aligned with the FinOps Foundation, a member of The Linux Foundation.

## What is FinOps?

FinOps is an operational framework and cultural practice that brings financial accountability to the variable spend model of cloud, enabling distributed teams to make business tradeoffs between speed, cost, and quality.

## Core Principles

### Collaboration
Teams need to work together: Finance, Technology, and Business units must collaborate to drive cloud financial management.

### Business Value
Decisions are driven by the business value of cloud, not just cost. Understanding the relationship between cost and value is crucial.

### Ownership
Everyone takes ownership for their cloud usage. Engineering teams are empowered to manage their own cloud costs.
