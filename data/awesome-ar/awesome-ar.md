## Overview

Awesome AR curates resources for augmented reality development including Apple ARKit, RealityKit, Google ARCore, Vuforia, WebXR, and other AR frameworks and tools.

## Features

- AR development frameworks (ARKit, ARCore, ARFoundation)
- WebAR libraries and tools for browser-based experiences
- 3D model creation and optimization tools
- Computer vision and tracking libraries
- Marker-based and markerless AR solutions
- SLAM and spatial mapping technologies
- AR cloud and persistent content platforms
- Hand tracking and gesture recognition
- AR glasses and headset development kits
- Sample projects and tutorials for beginners
