## Overview
The "Government Open Data Platform" (政府資料開放平臺) is Taiwan’s central government open data portal, referenced in the Awesome Public Datasets list for g0v-related government data. It provides access to official Taiwanese public sector datasets suitable for civic technology projects, research, and analysis.

## Features
- **Centralized government data portal** for Taiwan’s public sector open datasets.
- **Dataset service classification (資料集服務分類)** to help users browse and locate datasets.
- **Daily-updated datasets (每日異動資料集)** highlighting datasets with recent changes or additions.
- **Popular datasets section (熱門資料集)** showcasing frequently accessed datasets.
- **Accessibility-compliant interface** with certification indicating conformance to AA-level web accessibility standards.
- **Supports civic tech and analysis use cases** by providing machine-readable, reusable open data from government sources.
