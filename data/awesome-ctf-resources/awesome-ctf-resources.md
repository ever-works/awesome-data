## Overview

Awesome CTF Resources provides frameworks, libraries, resources and software for Capture The Flag competitions and security challenges.

## CTF Platforms

- **CTFlearn** - Platform for ethical hackers to learn, practice, and compete
- **Hacker101 CTF** - Platform by HackerOne for learning web security
- **HackTheBox** - Massive hacking playground
- **PicoCTF** - Beginner-friendly CTF competition
