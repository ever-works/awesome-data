# Awesome Public Datasets – Government (Meta: Finland)

**URL:** https://www.opendata.fi/en  
**Category:** Themed directories  
**Tags:** datasets, government, open-data  
**Source:** Listed in the *Awesome Public Datasets* catalog

---

## Overview

Finland’s national open data service (Suomi.fi Open Data / opendata.fi) is a centralized directory of governmental and public-sector datasets. It aggregates open data, APIs, and usage examples (showcases) from Finnish public bodies across a wide range of thematic areas.

---

## Features

### Thematic categories
Open data is organized into multiple subject areas with dataset counts (approximate, as listed):
- **Regions and Cities** – 429 datasets
- **Energy** – 34 datasets
- **International Issues** – 9 datasets
- **Education, Culture & Sport** – 160 datasets
- **Transport** – 168 datasets
- **Agriculture, Fisheries, Forestry and Food** – 8 datasets
- **Justice, Legal System and Public Safety** – 9 datasets
- **Built‑up Areas & Infrastructure** – 192 datasets
- **Economy and Finance** – 196 datasets
- **Health** – 67 datasets
- **Science and Technology** – 65 datasets
- **Population and Society** – 217 datasets
- **Government and Public Sector** – 233 datasets
- **Environment** – 177 datasets

Each category links to a page where users can browse all datasets in that group.

### Dataset and API directory
- **Datasets browser** – Central interface to search and browse all published open datasets.
- **APIs browser** – Dedicated section listing available APIs provided by public-sector bodies.
- **Showcases browser** – Collection of use cases and applications demonstrating how datasets and APIs are used.

### Data sharing support
- **Operating model for sharing data**  
  - Documentation and guidance on how Finnish public-sector organizations should share data.  
  - Available as a browsable “operating model” resource.
- **API principles**  
  - Guidance on recommended practices and principles for offering APIs.

### Access to restricted public data
- **Suomi.fi Restricted Data**  
  - A linked service for those seeking access to protected (non-open) data held by public-sector organizations.  
  - Accessible via a separate service endpoint: `https://suojattudata.suomi.fi`.

### News and updates
The service maintains a news section with articles on:
- Platform and category changes (e.g., introduction of a **high‑value dataset category**).
- Service and site improvements.
- Publisher and user stories describing real-world use of the service.
- Updates to documentation and tools (e.g., availability of the operating model in multiple languages, removal of third‑party commenting tools).

### Feeds and subscriptions
- **RSS feed** for subscribing to updates: `https://www.opendata.fi/en/rss.xml`.
