# Awesome Public Datasets – Government (Meta: Chicago)

## Overview
Awesome Public Datasets – Government (Meta: Chicago) is an entry in the broader *Awesome Public Datasets* collection that points to the City of Chicago’s official open data portal. It serves as a curated gateway to a wide range of municipal datasets covering multiple aspects of city operations and public life.

- **Type:** Themed data directory / meta entry
- **Focus:** Government open data from the City of Chicago
- **Source URL:** https://data.cityofchicago.org/
- **Category:** Themed directories
- **Tags:** datasets, government, open-data

- Curated reference to Chicago’s official open data portal

- Covers multiple domains such as:

  - Other municipal and civic data topics
- Part of an *awesome-style* catalog, aligning Chicago data with other notable public datasets
