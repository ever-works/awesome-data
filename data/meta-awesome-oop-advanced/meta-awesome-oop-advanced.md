## Overview
meta-awesome – OOP Advanced is a curated “directory of directories” that aggregates awesome lists and collections focused on advanced object-oriented programming (OOP) concepts, patterns, and related resources.

## Features
- Curated meta-list of advanced OOP resources
- Focus on higher-level OOP concepts and design, beyond basics
- Aggregates links to multiple “awesome” collections rather than individual articles
- Emphasis on design patterns, advanced patterns, and OOP best practices
- Suitable as an entry point for discovering specialized OOP resources
- Part of the broader **meta-awesome** project, which indexes many topic-specific awesome lists
- Tagging and categorization around:
  - `awesome-lists`
  - `oop`
  - `directory-of-directories`
