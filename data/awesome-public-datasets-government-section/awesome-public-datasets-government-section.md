## Overview
The **Awesome Public Datasets – Government Section** is a curated sub‑list within the broader Awesome Public Datasets ecosystem. It focuses specifically on **official government and intergovernmental open data portals** around the world.

It acts as a meta‑collection, pointing to high‑quality, authoritative data sources maintained by government agencies and organizations, making it useful for **research, analytics, data science projects, and open data applications**.

## Features
- **Government-focused scope**
  - Contains only public datasets and portals provided by **government** or **intergovernmental** bodies.
  - Emphasizes **official** and **authoritative** data sources.

- **Global coverage**
  - Includes portals and datasets from multiple **countries**, **national agencies**, and **cities**.
  - Covers international organizations such as **UNESCO** and other intergovernmental bodies.

- **Agency-level datasets**
  - References datasets and portals from agencies such as:
    - U.S. **FDA** (Food and Drug Administration)
    - U.S. **NCES** (National Center for Education Statistics)
    - **USPTO** (United States Patent and Trademark Office)
    - **UNESCO** (United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization)
    - National statistics bureaus and similar official statistical offices.

- **Open data catalogs**
  - Lists **national open data portals**.
  - Includes **city and regional open data catalogs**.
  - Targets resources that provide bulk downloads, APIs, and machine‑readable formats where available (via the underlying portals).

- **Curated “awesome” list structure**
  - Part of the **Awesome Public Datasets (APD)** core repository (`apd-core`).
  - Organized as a **GitHub-based markdown list**, allowing easy browsing, linking, and contributions.
  - Integrates within the wider Awesome ecosystem for discovery through tags and categories.

- **Use-case oriented utility**
  - Suitable for:
    - Academic and policy **research**.
    - **Data science** and **machine learning** projects.
    - **Civic tech** and **open government** initiatives.
    - **Analytics** and **business intelligence** involving official statistics and regulatory data.

- **Community-maintained**
  - Hosted on GitHub, enabling **community contributions, updates, and corrections** via pull requests and issues (following the Awesome/APD contribution guidelines).
