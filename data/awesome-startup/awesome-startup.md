## Overview

Awesome Startup provides a comprehensive collection of resources for building and growing a startup, including free tools, educational materials, legal templates, and practical guides covering all aspects of entrepreneurship from idea to scale.

## Key Categories

- **Learning Resources** - Courses, books, and educational content
- **Free Tools** - Productivity, development, and business tools
- **Legal Documents** - Templates for incorporation, agreements
- **Design Resources** - UI/UX tools and asset libraries
- **Marketing** - Growth hacking and marketing strategies
- **Fundraising** - Pitch decks, investor resources
- **Communities** - Founder networks and support groups
