## Overview

Awesome O11y Blogs catalogs the best blogs and thought leaders in the observability space, providing insights into monitoring, tracing, logging, and AIOps practices.

## Features

- Company engineering blogs (Netflix, Uber, Google SRE)
- Individual practitioners and thought leaders
- Open source project blogs (Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry)
- Observability platform vendor insights
- Deep dives into distributed tracing
- Logging best practices and patterns
- Metrics and monitoring strategies
- AIOps and machine learning for operations
- Cost optimization in observability
- Case studies and real-world implementations
