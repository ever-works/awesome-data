## Overview

Awesome Open Climate Science is a curated collection of open source software packages and resources dedicated to advancing climate science research. This repository serves the atmospheric, ocean, and climate science communities by providing access to cutting-edge computational tools.

## Key Areas

- **Atmospheric Science**: Tools for atmospheric modeling and analysis
- **Oceanography**: Software for ocean data processing and visualization
- **Climate Science**: Climate modeling and prediction frameworks
- **Hydrology**: Hydrological modeling and water cycle analysis

## Community

Part of the Pangeo project, which promotes open, reproducible, and scalable science for ocean, atmosphere, land, and climate research. The repository welcomes contributions from researchers and developers working on open source climate science tools.

## Licensing

All featured projects are open source, promoting transparency and collaboration in climate research.
