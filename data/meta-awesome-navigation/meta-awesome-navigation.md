## Overview
meta-awesome – Navigation is a curated meta-directory that aggregates “awesome” lists focused on navigation systems, routing, and related UI/UX components. It is part of the broader `meta-awesome` project, which organizes collections of high-quality resources into topic-specific documents.

## Features
- **Meta-aggregation of navigation lists**
  - Collects and links to multiple “awesome” lists specifically about navigation.
  - Focus on navigation systems, routing, and UI/UX patterns for moving through content or interfaces.

- **Navigation systems coverage**
  - Resources about implementing navigation structures in applications or websites.
  - Includes conceptual and practical materials around navigation design.

- **Routing-focused resources**
  - Lists related to routing libraries, frameworks, and patterns.
  - Emphasis on how users and data flow between views/pages in applications.

- **UI/UX navigation components**
  - Links to collections of components such as menus, sidebars, breadcrumbs, tabs, and similar interface patterns.
  - Focus on user experience considerations in how navigation is presented and interacted with.

- **Part of the `meta-awesome` ecosystem**
  - Integrates with other topic-specific meta-lists under the `meta-awesome` project.
  - Follows the “awesome list” style of community curation and organization.

## Use Cases
- Discovering new libraries, tools, or frameworks related to navigation and routing.
- Researching UI/UX best practices for application or website navigation.
- Quickly locating specialized “awesome” lists in the navigation domain.
