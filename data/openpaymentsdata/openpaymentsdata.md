# OpenPaymentsData

**Website:** https://openpaymentsdata.cms.gov  
**Category:** Themed Directories / Open Data Portal  
**Provider:** Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS)  
**Focus:** U.S. healthcare financial relationships between drug/medical device companies and healthcare providers

---

## Overview
OpenPaymentsData is the official CMS portal for accessing Open Payments data, which documents financial relationships between pharmaceutical/medical device manufacturers and healthcare providers and teaching hospitals in the United States. The site exposes detailed payment and ownership data to support transparency, research, and public oversight.

Data currently displayed covers **January 2018 – December 2024**.

---

## Features

### Data Access & Exploration
- **Search interface** to explore financial relationships by:
  - Individual provider
  - Teaching hospital
  - Company (drug or device manufacturer)
- **Provider profile search** with consolidated payment information for a specific provider.
- **Advanced Search** for more granular data queries.
- **Dataset Explorer** for browsing structured Open Payments datasets online.
- **Dataset Downloads** for bulk access to data files.
- **Archived Datasets** providing access to historical Open Payments data.

### Data Types & Categories
The portal covers a range of payment and value transfer scenarios, including examples such as:
- Hiring a physician to advise on a treatment in development
- Providing hospitals with promotional materials for a new drug or device
- Research grants to hospitals
- Paying healthcare professionals (e.g., nurses) to speak at conferences
- Meals or small transfers of value (e.g., lunch during a sales representative visit)
- Buying an ownership share in a company owned by a physician
- Donations to fund hospital expansions

Open Payments data is grouped into categories including:
- **Research payments**
- **General payments** (e.g., food, travel, consulting, speaking engagements, entertainment)
- **Ownership and investment interests** (held by physicians or immediate family members; not applicable to non‑physician practitioners or teaching hospitals)

An example distribution shown:
- 64% research payments
- 26% general payments
- 10% investments (ownership and investment interest)

### Summary & Insight Tools
- **Facts about Open Payments**: high-level explanations and summary statistics about the program and dataset.
- **Payments by State**: summary data aggregated at the state level.
- **Open Payments Insights**: high-level analytics such as payment purpose breakdowns and specialties with the highest aggregate payments (e.g., Orthopaedic Surgery, Internal Medicine, Endodontics, Dermatology, Neurology).

### Documentation & Reference

### API & Programmatic Access
- **API documentation** to access Open Payments data programmatically, enabling integration into research workflows, applications, and analyses.

### Governance & Compliance
