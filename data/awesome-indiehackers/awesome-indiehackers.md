## Overview

Awesome IndieHackers provides resources for independent creators who build and monetize projects without employees or external funding.

## Definition

An **indie hacker** is an independent creator who builds projects and makes money independently. They typically:
- Work solo or with a small team
- Bootstrap without venture capital
- Focus on sustainable revenue
- Build in public
- Share learning and metrics
