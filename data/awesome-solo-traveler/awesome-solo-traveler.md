## Overview

Awesome Solo Traveler provides comprehensive resources for individuals traveling alone, offering guidance, safety tips, communities, and tools to make solo travel rewarding and secure.

## Why Solo Travel?

- **Freedom**: Go where you want, when you want
- **Self-Discovery**: Personal growth through independent experiences
- **Flexibility**: Change plans spontaneously
- **Meeting People**: Easier to connect with locals and other travelers
- **Confidence Building**: Develop problem-solving and self-reliance skills

## Safety

### Safety Apps
- **bSafe**: Personal safety network
- **TripWhistle**: Emergency information
- **Sitata**: Travel warnings and alerts
- **GeoSure**: Real-time safety scores

### Safety Best Practices
- Share itinerary with trusted contacts
- Register with embassy
- Keep copies of documents
- Trust your instincts
- Stay aware of surroundings
- Use reputable accommodations

## Accommodation

### Budget Options
- **Hostelworld**: Hostel bookings (great for meeting others)
- **Couchsurfing**: Free stays with locals
- **Workaway/HelpX**: Exchange work for accommodation

### Mid-Range
- **Airbnb**: Entire places or private rooms
- **Booking.com**: Hotels and apartments
- **Selina**: Coliving spaces for travelers

### Solo-Friendly Hostels
Look for:
- Common areas for socializing
- Organized activities
- Female-only dorms (if preferred)
- Lockers and security
