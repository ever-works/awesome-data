### Awesome Public Datasets – Finance / NASDAQ Data

**Source:** [Nasdaq Data Link](https://data.nasdaq.com/)  
**Category:** Themed directories  
**Tags:** datasets, financial, awesome-lists

---

## Overview
Nasdaq Data Link is a directory and delivery platform for financial, economic and alternative datasets. It aggregates and distributes curated market and fundamental data products used by financial institutions, researchers and analysts.

---

## Features
- **Financial & Market Data Catalog**
  - Centralized catalog of market, economic, and alternative datasets.
  - Focus on datasets with “actionable information” for investment and risk analysis.

- **Data Discovery & Access**
  - Web catalog for browsing available datasets.
  - Ability to access, trial and test data before subscribing.
  - Sample data available for many premium datasets.

- **Account-Based Access**
  - Free account creation to get started.
  - Step-by-step onboarding flow:
    1. Create an account (free).
    2. Access and trial data products.
    3. Use data to build and test strategies.

- **Example Data Products (Premium)**
  - **Nasdaq Basic** (`/databases/NB`)
    - Best bid and offer quotations from the Nasdaq execution system.
    - Trade data from the Nasdaq execution system.
    - Trade data from the FINRA/Nasdaq Trade Reporting Facility (TRF).
    - Premium dataset with sample data available.
  - **Equities 360** (`/databases/E360`)
    - Fundamental data: income statement, balance sheet, cash flow and more.
    - Company reference data.
    - Pricing data and corporate actions.
    - Analytics built on top of the above datasets.
    - Premium dataset with sample data available.
  - **Nasdaq Fund Network Data Service** (`/databases/NFN`)
    - Historical and daily fund-level valuation data.
    - Dividend and capital distribution reports.
    - Daily list coverage for 90,000+ investment instruments from the Nasdaq Fund Network.
    - Premium dataset with sample data available.
  - **Earnings Quality Ranking (EQR)** (`/databases/EQR`)
    - Weekly rankings of relative earnings quality for a large universe of publicly traded US equities.
    - Uses accounting metrics to compare companies to their industry peers.
    - Ranks from 1 (low earnings quality) to 5 or 99 (high earnings quality).
    - Premium dataset with sample data available.
  - **Marex Daily Speculative Positioning (MDSP)**
    - Estimates aggregate net position of speculative trades in commodities futures markets on a daily basis.
    - Designed to provide a faster, more timely signal than traditional commitment reports.

- **Data Infrastructure (Data Fabric)**
  - Underlying data infrastructure technology used to power Nasdaq Data Link globally.
  - Offered as **Data Fabric** to help organizations manage their proprietary data.
  - Focus on flexible, scalable, simple-to-use and reliable data operations.
