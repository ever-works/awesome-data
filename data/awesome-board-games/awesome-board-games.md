# Awesome Board Games

A curated, open-source directory of notable tabletop and board games, with descriptions and references for people interested in board gaming.

- **Website**: https://github.com/edm00se/awesome-board-games#readme  
- **Category**: Themed Directories  
- **Tags**: awesome-lists, games, fun

---

## Overview

Awesome Board Games is a community-maintained list that highlights a wide range of tabletop and board games. Each entry typically links to external resources (such as BoardGameGeek) and includes thematic descriptions, player counts, approximate playtime, and age recommendations where available.

The list is organized into categories like:
- **Family** – Accessible games suitable for mixed ages and casual play
- **Strategy** – More complex titles emphasizing planning and tactics
- **Party** – Social and high-player-count games

There is also guidance on how to **contribute** new games or improvements to the list.

## Features

### 1. Structured Game Directory
- Curated collection of tabletop and board games.  
- Each game typically includes:
  - Title and link (often to BoardGameGeek)  
  - Thematic/setting description  
  - Player count (min/max)  
  - Recommended minimum age  
  - Approximate playtime
- Organized by gameplay style and audience (e.g., Family, Strategy, Party).

### 2. Family Games (Examples from the List)

#### Takenoko
- **Theme**: Tending a bamboo garden for the Japanese Emperor’s panda, originally gifted by the Chinese Emperor as a symbol of peace.
- **Core Gameplay**:
  - Cultivate land plots.
  - Irrigate fields.
  - Grow three species of bamboo: Green, Yellow, Pink.
  - Use the Imperial gardener to manage the bamboo garden.
  - Balance bamboo growth with the panda’s hunger for bamboo.
- **Objective**: Manage land plots most effectively, grow the most bamboo, and satisfy the panda’s appetite to score the most points.
- **Player Count**: 2–4
- **Minimum Age**: 8+
- **Playtime**: ~45 minutes

#### Shadows of Brimstone: Swamps of Death
- **Theme**: Old West dungeon-crawl with horror elements.
- **Core Gameplay**:
  - Fully cooperative board game.
  - Players create characters from classic Western hero archetypes (e.g., Law Man, Gunslinger, Saloon Girl).
  - Form a posse to explore dark mines infested with demons and otherworldly creatures.
  - Tactical combat with heavy dice usage.
  - Robust, card-driven exploration system ensuring high replayability.
  - Discover new enemies, loot, and hazards as you progress.
  - Find and traverse portals to other worlds to continue adventures.
- **Swamps of Death Specifics**:
  - Introduces the otherworld **Jargono**, a humid swampland.
  - Features sentient reptiles, vicious dinosaurs, and a stranded humanoid tribe whose return portal closed long ago.

### 3. Category-Based Browsing
- Users can explore games by category:
  - **Family** – Lighter rules, broader age suitability.
  - **Strategy** – Greater depth, planning, and complexity.
  - **Party** – Social interaction, larger groups.
- Table-of-contents navigation makes it easy to jump between sections.

### 4. External References
- Each game links to an external details page (commonly BoardGameGeek), allowing readers to:
  - Read extended rules and reviews.
  - View component images and player discussions.
  - Check expansions and variants.

### 5. Community Contributions
- The project is hosted on GitHub as an “awesome list,” encouraging:
  - Community submissions of new games.
  - Improvements to descriptions and categorization.
  - Maintenance and expansion over time via pull requests.
