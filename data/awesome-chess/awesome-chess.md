# Awesome Chess

**URL:** https://github.com/hkirat/awesome-chess#readme

## Overview
Awesome Chess is a curated directory of chess-related resources collected from across the internet. It focuses on libraries, tools, engines, and references useful for developers, researchers, and enthusiasts working with chess.

## Features
- **Curated resource list**
  - Organized collection of chess-related assets available online
  - Focus on usefulness for development and research contexts
- **Books**
  - Reading material related to chess (theory, practice, or analysis)
- **FEN Parsers**

  - Enable description, storage, and processing of specific board positions
- **Bots**
  - Chess-playing algorithms and AI bots for playing against users or other engines
- **Move Validators**
  - Tools to check the legality and authenticity of chess moves
  - Useful for game validation and rules enforcement
- **Websites**
  - Online platforms for playing chess
  - May include learning or analysis sites (as part of the curated list)
- **Boards**
  - Tools and libraries for rendering chess boards (e.g., UI components)
- **Board Notations**
  - Resources for representing chess boards and moves in textual formats
  - Includes links such as a tutorial on chess notation
- **Pieces**
  - Artwork and graphical assets for chess pieces
  - Useful for building interfaces or visualizations
- **Talks**
  - Recorded talks and presentations related to chess
- **Open contribution model**
  - Public contribution guidelines for adding or improving entries
  - Community-maintained list hosted on GitHub
