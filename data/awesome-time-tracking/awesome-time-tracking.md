# awesome-time-tracking

**Category:** themed-directories  
**Brand:** ever-works  
**Source:** https://github.com/ever-works/awesome-time-tracking

## Description
awesome-time-tracking is a curated, open list of time tracking resources, including applications, libraries, tools, and practices related to tracking time and productivity. It is part of the broader awesome-lists ecosystem and maintained by ever-works.

## Features
- Curated directory of time tracking software, libraries, and tools
- Organized by use case and domain-specific needs
- Links out to detailed entries in subfolders (e.g., `details/`)
- Openly maintained on GitHub with community visibility (stars, forks)
- Licensed under CC-BY-SA-4.0

### Covered Categories
The list is structured into multiple sections, including:
- **Attendance & Payroll Management** – tools for tracking work hours and supporting payroll workflows
- **Team & Project Time Tracking** – solutions for teams and project-based time logging
- **Automated Time Tracking** – tools that track time with minimal manual input
- **Desktop Time Tracking Applications** – native desktop apps for time tracking
- **Field Team Management** – time tracking for mobile/field-based teams
- **Mobile Time Tracking** – mobile-first apps for on-the-go tracking
- **Resource Scheduling** – tools that combine scheduling with time tracking
- **Business Management** – broader business tools that incorporate time tracking
- **Commerce** – time-related tools tied to commercial or billing workflows
- **Countdown & Interval Timers** – timers for sprints, intervals, and countdown-based work
- **Cross-Platform Time Tracking** – tools available across multiple operating systems/devices
- **Developer Time Tracking** – time tracking tailored specifically to developers
- **Equipment & Fleet Time Tracking** – tracking time usage of equipment and fleets
- **Open Sources** – open-source time tracking tools and libraries
- **Others** – miscellaneous time-related tools that don’t fit other categories
- **Personal Time Tracking** – tools for individuals tracking personal productivity or habits
- **Practices** – methodologies and best practices for time tracking
- **Productivity Tools** – broader productivity tools related to time management
- **Time Tracking APIs & Integrations** – APIs and integrations to connect time tracking with other systems
