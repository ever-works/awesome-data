# Indie

**Description**  
Curated “awesome list” of resources to help independent developers earn money from their own digital products and businesses (e.g., monetizing side projects, bootstrapping small products, building indie income streams).

**Website**  
https://github.com/mezod/awesome-indie#readme

**Category**  
Themed Directories

**Tags**  
startups, indie, business-tools

---

## Features

- Curated collection focused specifically on **independent developers** and solo/bootstrapped founders.
- Resources oriented around **making money from code and digital products**, including:
  - Monetizing side projects
  - Bootstrapping small, profitable products
  - Building independent and/or passive income streams
- Emphasis on **small, profitable projects** rather than large-scale startups.
- Content designed to **inspire and guide** developers toward:
  - Launching products
  - Experimenting with ideas
  - Iterating and “making and trying stuff”
- Includes a structured **table of contents** (e.g., “Communities” section, with others in the full README such as tools, guides, etc.).
- Lists **communities** relevant to indie developers, including:
  - IndieHackers – interviews and learnings from profitable businesses and side projects.
  - bootstrapped.fm Forum – discussion forum for bootstrappers.
  - `##passiveincome` on Freenode – IRC channel focused on passive income.
  - (More communities and sections are available in the full repository README.)
- Open to **community contributions**, with guidelines provided in `CONTRIBUTION.md`.
- Licensed repository (license details in `LICENSE` file).
