# Awesome Public Datasets – Economics: UN Commodity Trade Statistics

**Category:** Themed directories  
**Brand:** awesome-public-datasets  
**Source:** [GitHub – APD Core: UN Commodity Trade Statistics](https://github.com/awesomedata/apd-core/tree/master/core//Economics/UN-Commodity-Trade-Statistics.yml)

## Overview
Awesome Public Datasets – Economics: UN Commodity Trade Statistics is a curated directory entry pointing to the UN Comtrade database, which provides detailed UN Commodity Trade Statistics for analyzing international merchandise trade.

## Features
- **Curated directory entry** within the Awesome Public Datasets (APD) project.
- **Focus on economics and trade**, specifically international merchandise trade.
- **Links to UN Comtrade database**, a primary source of official UN Commodity Trade Statistics.
- **Supports trade analysis use cases**, such as:
  - Studying bilateral trade flows between countries.
  - Analyzing imports and exports by commodity.
  - Exploring trends in international merchandise trade over time.
- **Standardized metadata via YAML** in the APD core repository, enabling:
  - Programmatic discovery and cataloging of the dataset.
  - Consistent categorization under “Economics/UN Commodity Trade Statistics”.

## Data & Coverage
- **Type of data:** International merchandise trade statistics.
- **Source organization:** United Nations (via UN Comtrade).
- **Typical variables** (implied from UN Comtrade context):
  - Trade values and quantities.
  - Reporter and partner countries.
  - Commodity codes (e.g., HS or other classification schemes).
  - Time period (usually annual or more frequent).

## Use Cases
- Economic research on global trade patterns.
- Policy analysis and impact assessment related to trade.
- Market analysis and competitive intelligence.
- Academic teaching and data-driven coursework in international economics.
