## Overview

Awesome XR encompasses the entire spectrum of extended reality technologies, providing resources for developers working across VR, AR, and MR platforms and devices.

## Features

- Cross-platform XR frameworks (Unity XR, Unreal Engine)
- WebXR for browser-based immersive experiences
- Native platform SDKs (OpenXR, Oculus SDK, SteamVR)
- 3D asset creation and optimization tools
- Haptic feedback and controller input libraries
- Social VR and multiplayer frameworks
- 360-degree video and spatial audio tools
- XR interaction design patterns
- Performance profiling and optimization guides
- Accessibility features for inclusive XR experiences
