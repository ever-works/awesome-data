## Overview

**Meta Awesome - Presentation** is a curated, Awesome-inspired meta list that aggregates other Awesome-style directories, collections, and resource lists focused on creating and delivering presentations. It serves as a central index to help users discover high-quality lists about presentation tools, slide design, speaking skills, and related presentation resources.

## Features

- **Meta index of presentation-related Awesome lists**  
  Aggregates links to multiple Awesome and similar curated lists specifically about presentations.

- **Focus on presentation creation and delivery**  
  Targets resources around slide creation, visual design, storytelling, public speaking, and presenting techniques.

- **Directory of directories**  
  Acts as a meta-directory, pointing to other directories and resource compilations rather than individual tools alone.

- **Community-driven curation**  
  Hosted on GitHub, allowing the open-source community to suggest additions, updates, and improvements via pull requests and issues.

- **Consistent Awesome-list style**  
  Follows the structure and philosophy of Awesome lists (clear categorization, concise descriptions, emphasis on quality resources).

- **Open and freely accessible**  
  Available as a public markdown document in a GitHub repository, readable without special tools.

## Use Cases

- Finding comprehensive lists of tools and resources for designing slide decks.
- Discovering curated guides for improving public speaking and presentation skills.
- Exploring specialized Awesome lists for presentation frameworks, libraries, or slide generators.
- Using as a starting point to build or contribute to your own presentation-related Awesome list.
