# Awesome Music

**Website:** https://github.com/ciconia/awesome-music#readme  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** awesome-lists, media, audio

## Overview
Awesome Music is a curated, categorized directory of music-related frameworks, libraries, tools, software, and resources focused on music creation, analysis, and processing.

## Features
- **Curated Awesome List**
  - Focused on music technology and digital audio
  - Community-maintained, with contribution guidelines and quality standards

- **Audio Editing**
  - Links to software and tools for editing and manipulating audio

- **Audio Libraries**
  - Collections of programming libraries for working with audio data
  - Intended for developers building audio and music applications

- **Audio Tools**
  - Standalone utilities and applications for audio processing and analysis

- **Free Music Scores**
  - Resources for obtaining music scores at no cost

- **MIDI Tools & Libraries**
  - Tools for working with MIDI data
  - Programming libraries for MIDI integration in music software

- **Music Notation**
  - Software and libraries for music engraving and notation handling

- **Music Programming**
  - Languages, environments, and frameworks for algorithmic composition and music-related coding

- **Music Theory Teaching**
  - Educational tools and resources for learning and teaching music theory

- **Standards**
  - References to relevant standards used in music and audio technology

- **Related Awesome Lists**
  - Links to other thematically related lists within the broader Awesome ecosystem
