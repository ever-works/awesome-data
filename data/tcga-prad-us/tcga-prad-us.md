# TCGA-PRAD-US

The TCGA Prostate Adenocarcinoma (PRAD) cohort (499 samples) available in cBioPortal and indexed in the Awesome Public Datasets collection. It provides genomic and clinical data for prostate adenocarcinoma, widely used for cancer genomics research and benchmarking pipelines.

[http://www.cbioportal.org/study?id=prad_tcga](http://www.cbioportal.org/study?id=prad_tcga)
