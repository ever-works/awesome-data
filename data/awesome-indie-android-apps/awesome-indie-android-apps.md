## Overview

Awesome Indie Android Apps celebrates mobile applications built by independent developers, providing inspiration and showcasing what's possible for solo creators.

## Features

- Curated collection of successful indie Android apps
- Timeline of app launches and updates
- Revenue and download statistics where available
- Development stories and lessons learned
- Marketing and distribution strategies
- Monetization approaches (ads, IAP, premium)
- Tech stacks and frameworks used
- Design patterns for solo development
- App Store Optimization (ASO) techniques
- Community feedback and reviews

## Pricing

Free and open-source.
