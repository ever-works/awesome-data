## Overview

Awesome AR/VR Research curates academic papers and tools focused on the systems and networking aspects of augmented and virtual reality, covering latency, bandwidth, edge computing, and more.

## Features

- Network optimization for real-time AR/VR streaming
- Edge computing architectures for low-latency experiences
- Bandwidth reduction and video compression techniques
- Motion-to-photon latency reduction research
- Multi-user AR/VR networking protocols
- Cloud rendering and streaming technologies
- Wireless VR and untethered systems
- Power efficiency for mobile AR/VR
- QoS and QoE metrics for immersive experiences
- Academic conferences and publication venues
