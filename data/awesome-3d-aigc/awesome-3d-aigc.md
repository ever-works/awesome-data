## Overview

A comprehensive collection of research papers and open-source resources focused on 3D AI-Generated Content (AIGC), covering 3D Gaussian splatting, neural rendering, mesh generation, and scene synthesis.

## Features

- 3D Gaussian splatting techniques and implementations
- Neural radiance fields (NeRF) and variants
- Text-to-3D generation methods
- Image-to-3D reconstruction
- 3D scene generation and editing
- Texture synthesis and material generation
- Physics-based 3D generation

## Key Technologies

### 3D Gaussian Splatting

- Explicit scene representations
- Real-time rendering capabilities
- Differentiable rendering algorithms
- High-quality novel view synthesis

### Text-to-3D

- DreamFusion-style approaches
- Score distillation sampling
- Multi-view diffusion models
- Geometry and texture generation

### Image-to-3D

- Single-image 3D reconstruction
- Multi-view stereo with AI
- Depth estimation and 3D lifting

## Recent Advances (2025-2026)

- Step1X-3D for scalable 3D generation
- Improved Gaussian splatting techniques
- Real-time 3D generation systems
- Physics-aware 3D synthesis

## Applications

- Game asset creation
- Virtual reality content
- Digital twin generation
- Product visualization
