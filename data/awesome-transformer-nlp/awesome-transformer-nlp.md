## Overview

Awesome Transformer NLP is a comprehensive collection of resources specifically focused on transformer architectures and their applications in natural language processing. This curated list covers everything from foundational papers to practical implementations.

## Features

- **Transformer Architecture Resources**: Complete coverage of attention mechanisms, positional encoding, and transformer variants
- **Large Language Models**: Resources for GPT-3, GPT-4, BERT, T5, and other state-of-the-art models
- **Implementation Guides**: Tutorials and code examples for implementing transformers from scratch
- **Pre-trained Models**: Links to downloadable models and model hubs
- **Research Papers**: Curated collection of seminal and recent papers in transformer research
- **Transfer Learning**: Resources on fine-tuning and adapting transformers for specific tasks
- **Tools and Frameworks**: Coverage of Hugging Face Transformers, PyTorch, TensorFlow implementations
- **Applications**: Examples across machine translation, text generation, question answering, and more

## Categories Covered

- Attention mechanisms and self-attention
- Encoder-decoder architectures
- Bidirectional and autoregressive models
- Vision transformers
- Multimodal transformers
- Efficient transformers and optimization techniques
- Prompt engineering and in-context learning

## Resources

Includes tutorials, Colab notebooks, video lectures, blog posts, and community discussions around transformer-based NLP.
