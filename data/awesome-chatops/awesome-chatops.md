# Awesome ChatOps

**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** awesome-lists, devops, communication

## Overview
Awesome ChatOps is a curated, open-source directory of resources about ChatOps—a collaboration model that connects people, processes, tools, and automation into conversation-driven workflows. It focuses on how teams can use chat platforms to automate tasks, coordinate operations, and improve transparency.

Source: <https://github.com/exAspArk/awesome-chatops#readme>

## What is ChatOps?
ChatOps is a way of working where:
- Operations and business tasks are executed and automated directly from chat tools
- People, processes, and tooling are connected in a shared, conversational interface
- Teams collaborate in a transparent, logged environment, aiming to work more efficiently and consistently
