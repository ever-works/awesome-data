## Overview
awesome-torch is a curated, community-maintained “awesome list” that aggregates tutorials, projects, libraries, and other resources from across the Torch ecosystem. It focuses on machine learning and deep learning workflows built with Torch and related Python tooling.

## Features
- **Curated Torch Ecosystem List**
  - Collects and organizes links related to the Torch framework and its ecosystem.
  - Emphasis on quality, relevance, and usefulness for practitioners.

- **Tutorials and Learning Resources**
  - Guides and tutorials for learning Torch from beginner to advanced levels.
  - Educational materials focused on deep learning, model building, and experimentation with Torch.

- **Projects and Example Implementations**
  - References to open-source Torch-based projects.
  - Example implementations of models and techniques built with Torch.

- **Libraries and Tools**
  - Links to Torch-compatible libraries, extensions, and utilities.
  - Python-based tooling that integrates with or extends the Torch ecosystem.

- **Community and Ecosystem Resources**
  - Pointers to community-driven content such as repos, collections, or other lists related to Torch.
  - Acts as a hub for discovering active work and shared knowledge in the Torch community.
