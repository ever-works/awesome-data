## Overview

Startup Stash is a well-established curated directory that catalyzes startups and entrepreneurs by aggregating essential tools and resources in one searchable platform.

## Features

- **Curated Categories**: Design tools, marketing resources, development frameworks, business tools, and more
- **Free Submission**: Submit your tool for consideration at no cost
- **Selective Curation**: Quality-focused review process
- **Strong SEO**: Established domain authority provides valuable backlinks
- **Search & Discovery**: Tools organized by category for easy browsing

## Submission Process

- Free submission available
- Selective curation process ensures quality
- Best suited for established tools with proven value
- May take time for review and approval
- Not ideal for experimental or very niche products

## Benefits

- Strong domain authority for SEO
- Long-term visibility and discoverability
- Credibility boost from being featured
- Targeted audience of entrepreneurs and startup founders

## Target Audience

Startup founders, entrepreneurs, and product managers looking for tools to build and grow their businesses.

## Pricing

Free submission, selective approval process.
