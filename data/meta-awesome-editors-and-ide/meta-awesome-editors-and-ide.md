# Meta Awesome – Editors and IDE

**Website:** https://github.com/jepemo/meta-awesome/blob/master/doc/editors-and-ide.md  
**Category:** Meta directories  
**Tags:** editor, developer-tools, directory-of-directories

## Overview
Meta Awesome – Editors and IDE is a curated sub-list within the broader **meta-awesome** collection. It serves as an index of “awesome lists” specifically focused on code editors and integrated development environments (IDEs). Instead of listing tools directly, it points developers to specialized awesome lists that cover different editors and IDE ecosystems.

## Features
- **Meta-directory focus**
  - Organizes and links to multiple “awesome” collections related to editors and IDEs.
  - Functions as a directory-of-directories for development tooling.

- **Editor & IDE specialization**
  - Targets tools developers use to write, edit, and manage source code.
  - Covers both standalone text editors and full IDEs.

- **Curated awesome lists**
  - Indexes existing awesome lists rather than individual plugins or tools.
  - Helps users quickly discover community-maintained resources per editor/IDE.

- **Part of meta-awesome ecosystem**
  - Integrated into the larger meta-awesome repository that organizes awesome lists by topic.
  - Benefits from the community conventions and structure of the awesome-list ecosystem.

- **Developer-oriented navigation**
  - Designed for developers looking to compare, explore, or deepen their use of particular editors/IDEs.
  - Useful starting point for finding plugins, themes, and workflows via the referenced awesome lists.

## Use Cases
- Discovering comprehensive awesome lists for a specific editor or IDE.
- Exploring alternative editors/IDEs and their ecosystems.
- Bootstrapping research on extensions, themes, and productivity tools via the linked awesome lists.
