## Overview

Awesome Fintech India specifically catalogs financial technology resources available in the Indian market, including UPI, NEFT/RTGS, and India-specific payment and banking solutions.

## Features

- UPI (Unified Payments Interface) integration libraries
- Payment gateways (Razorpay, Paytm, Cashfree, PhonePe)
- Banking APIs from major Indian banks
- NEFT, RTGS, and IMPS transaction tools
- Aadhaar and eKYC verification services
- GST and tax compliance APIs
- Digital lending and NBFC platforms
- Account aggregator framework resources
- Mutual funds and investment APIs
- Insurance and InsurTech platforms
