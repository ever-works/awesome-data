## Empathy in Engineering

**Tags:** culture, engineering, community

### Overview
Empathy in Engineering is an "awesome"-style, community-maintained collection of resources focused on building and promoting more compassionate and empathetic engineering cultures.

### Features
- Curated list of resources centered on empathy and compassion in engineering teams
- Focus on cultural aspects of software engineering (not tools or products)
- Community-oriented: intended to be extended and maintained collaboratively
- Organized in the spirit of other "Awesome" lists, emphasizing quality and relevance

### Pricing
- Not a product or paid service; it is an open, public GitHub resource available free of charge.
