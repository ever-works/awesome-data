## Features

- **Curated OCaml ecosystem index**  
  - Organized list of OCaml tools, frameworks, libraries, and educational resources.  
  - Community-driven: contributions accepted via pull requests.

- **Learning and reference material**  
  - Dedicated collections of freely available:  
    - **Books** (OCaml learning and reference)  
    - **Papers** (research and academic material related to OCaml)  
    - **Presentations** (talks, slides, and similar resources)  
  - Links to:  
    - **OCamlverse** for broader, community-driven OCaml content.  
    - **“Up and Running with OCaml”** tutorial for a quick introduction to modern OCaml workflows.
