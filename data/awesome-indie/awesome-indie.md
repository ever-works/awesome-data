## Overview

Awesome Indie is a curated collection of resources specifically for independent developers (indie hackers) who want to build profitable businesses and generate income from their side projects without relying on traditional funding or employment.

## What is an Indie Hacker?

An indie hacker is an independent creator who builds projects and makes money independently, without employees or external funding, focusing on sustainable, profitable businesses.

## Resource Categories
