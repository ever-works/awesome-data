## Overview

Awesome SRE Observability Automation brings together resources that intersect site reliability engineering, observability practices, and automation techniques for reliable systems at scale.

## Features

- SLO and SLI definitions and implementations
- Error budgets and toil tracking
- Auto-remediation and self-healing systems
- Chaos engineering and resilience testing
- Predictive analytics for system reliability
- Automated incident response playbooks
- Infrastructure as Code for observability
- Configuration management and drift detection
- Capacity planning automation
- Cost optimization through automation
