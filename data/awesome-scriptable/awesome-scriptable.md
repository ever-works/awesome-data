# Awesome Scriptable

**URL:** https://github.com/dersvenhesse/awesome-scriptable#readme  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** iOS, Automation, JavaScript  
**Brand:** awesome

## Overview
Awesome Scriptable is a curated, Awesome-style directory of scripts and widgets for Scriptable, the iOS JavaScript automation app. It organizes community-created widgets and examples into topical categories, helping users discover automation ideas and ready‑to‑use scripts for their iOS devices.

## Features
- **Curated awesome list format**
  - Follows the "Awesome" list convention for structure and quality.
  - Focuses on “awesome Scriptable widgets” specifically.

- **Scriptable-focused content**
  - All entries are built for [Scriptable](https://scriptable.app/), an app that lets users automate iOS with JavaScript.
  - Items include widgets, scripts, and examples demonstrating Scriptable capabilities.

- **Topical categorization of widgets and scripts**
  - **Art** – e.g., *Random-Canvas* (generates random images).
  - **Cars** – widgets and scripts related to vehicles and driving (category stub shown in contents).
  - **Calendar** – date and scheduling-related widgets.
  - **Finance** – tracking markets, budgets, and financial data.
  - **Food** – food, recipes, and related utilities.
  - **Gaming** – game-related stats, integrations, and utilities.
  - **Health** – health and fitness related widgets.
  - **Mobile** – phone and device utilities.
  - **Music** – music playback, charts, or service integrations.
  - **Reading** – reading lists, articles, or book-related widgets.
  - **Social** – social media related widgets and stats.
  - **Shopping** – shopping lists, price checks, and deal tracking.
  - **Space** – astronomy and space data (e.g., NASA feeds, ISS tracking, etc.).
  - **Sports** – sports scores and statistics.
  - **Tools** – general utilities, helpers, and productivity widgets.
  - **Traveling** – travel, transit, and location-related widgets.

- **Related resources section**
  - **Download helper** – tools or scripts that simplify downloading or installing Scriptable widgets from the list.
  - **Additional resources** – links to more Scriptable-related material beyond the main widget list.
  - **Articles** – educational or informational articles about Scriptable and building widgets.

- **Open contribution model**
  - Public `contributing.md` file that defines how to propose new entries or improvements.
  - Community-driven: anyone can suggest new widgets, tools, or resources to be added.

- **License**
  - Licensed under **CC0-1.0** (public domain dedication), enabling broad reuse of the list content.
