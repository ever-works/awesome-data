## Overview

Awesome Public Datasets – Government: Japan points to Japan’s official government statistics and open data portal (e-Stat). The portal aggregates a wide range of public datasets from Japanese government ministries and agencies, suitable for research, policy analysis, and data-driven projects. It provides multiple ways to browse and access official statistics, including thematic, organizational, and alphabetical navigation, as well as database views.

---

## Features

### 1. Browsing Statistics

- **All Statistics View**  
  - Central entry point to search and browse all available statistics.

- **Statistics by Theme**  
  Pre-organized into thematic categories:
  - Population and Households
  - Labor and Wages
  - Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries
  - Mining and Manufacturing
  - Domestic Trade and Services
  - Business, Household, Economy
  - Housing, Estate and Construction
  - Energy and Water
  - Transport and Sightseeing
  - Information and Communication, Science and Technology
  - Education, Culture, Sports and Life
  - Administration and Public Finance
  - Justice, Security and Environment
  - Social Security and Sanitation
  - International
  - Others

- **Statistics by Organization**  
  Browse datasets by the producing ministry/agency, including:
  - Cabinet Office
  - National Public Safety Commission and National Police Agency
  - Ministry of Internal Affairs and Communications
  - Ministry of Finance of Japan
  - Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology
  - Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare
  - Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries
  - Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry
  - Agency of Natural Resources and Energy
  - Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, Transport and Tourism

- **Fundamental Statistics**  
  - Dedicated view for key / fundamental national statistics (e.g., major official surveys and censuses).

- **Statistics A–Z**  
  Alphabetical browsing of statistics:
  - Direct access by initial letter (A, B, C, E, F, H, I, L, M, N, O, P, Q, R, S, T, U, V, etc.).
  - Supports quick lookup when the dataset name is known.

### 2. Database Access

- **Database View**  
  - Separate **Database** section for working with statistics in a more structured / tabular form.

- **Database by Theme**  
  Thematic access within the database interface, including (as shown in content):
  - Population and Households
  - Labor and Wages
  - Mining and Manufacturing
  - Domestic Trade and Services
  - Business, Household, Economy
  - Housing, Estate and Construction
  - Information and Communication, Science and Technology (partially shown in content, implied full thematic coverage similar to non-database view)

### 3. Navigation & Discovery

- Multiple parallel navigation paths:
  - By **theme** (subject area)
  - By **organization** (ministry/agency)
  - **Fundamental** core datasets
  - **A–Z** index
  - **All** statistics consolidated search/list
- English-language entry points (via `/en/` paths) for international users.

### 4. Data Scope & Types

*(Inferred from the categories listed)*

- Demographic and social datasets (population, households, education, culture, sports, life).
- Labor market and wage statistics.
- Sectoral economic data (agriculture, forestry, fisheries, mining, manufacturing, trade, services, housing, construction, energy, water, transport, tourism, information and communication, science and technology).
- Public finance and administration statistics.
- Justice, security, and environmental indicators.
- Social security, health, and sanitation data.
- Internationally-oriented statistics (e.g., comparisons, trade, external relations) and miscellaneous categories.
