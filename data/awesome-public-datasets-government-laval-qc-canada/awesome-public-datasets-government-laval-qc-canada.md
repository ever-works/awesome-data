# Awesome Public Datasets – Government: Laval, QC, Canada

## Overview
Awesome Public Datasets links to the City of Laval’s official municipal portal for local government information and resources. While the referenced entry is intended to surface open government datasets, the visible page content focuses on municipal policies and action plans rather than a structured data catalog.

- **Name:** Awesome Public Datasets – Government: Laval, QC, Canada  
- **Type:** Directory entry pointing to a municipal government site  
- **Source URL:** http://www.laval.ca/Pages/Fr/Citoyens/donnees.aspx  
- **Category:** Themed directories  
- **Tags:** datasets, government, open-data

## Features

### General municipal information
- Provides access to various municipal resources and information for residents of Laval, QC.  
- Includes a **“Recherche par adresse” (Search by address)** tool to retrieve localized municipal information such as:
  - Collection days (e.g., waste/recycling pickup schedules)
  - Watering schedules
  - Nearest ecocentre address
  - Name of the elected representative for a given district

### Policies and action plans (documents)
The portal segment shown focuses on **“Politiques et plans d’action”** (policies and action plans) of the City of Laval, providing downloadable documents (often PDFs) that guide municipal decision-making.

#### Declaration of services to citizens
- **Document:** Déclaration de services aux citoyennes et citoyens (approx. 271 KB)  
- Describes the City’s service commitments to residents across communication channels (online, telephone, mail, in-person).  
- Outlines quality-of-service principles the administration aims to uphold when serving citizens.

#### Active aging action plan 2025–2030
- **Documents:**
  - Plan d’action en matière de vieillissement actif 2025-2030 (approx. 2.45 MB)
  - Plan d’action en matière de vieillissement actif 2025-2030 – accessible version (approx. 557 KB)
- Strategic framework aligned with the vision **“Laval 2035 : urbaine de nature”**.  
- Main orientations:
  - Promote health, autonomy, and social participation of older adults.
  - Create inclusive, accessible, safe, and welcoming living environments for all generations.
  - Recognize and value the contribution of seniors to the community.  
- Confirms Laval’s positioning and commitments as a **“Municipalité amie des aînés”** (Age-Friendly Municipality).

#### Heritage action plan 2020–2024
- **Document:** Plan d’action en patrimoine 2020-2024 (approx. 450 KB)  
- Objective: Protect, showcase, and promote Laval’s cultural and built heritage.  
- Based on an extensive consultation process with citizens and local cultural organizations within the 2017–2019 regional cultural development plan.  
- Structures municipal actions around four main axes (high-level guidance for heritage policy).  
- Includes a set of **45 concrete actions** to implement heritage-related goals.

##### Progress tracking (as of 31 August 2023)
- The site reports on the state of advancement of the 45 actions in the heritage plan.
- Example actions under **“Élargissement de la connaissance du patrimoine lavallois”** (broadening knowledge of Laval’s heritage):
  - **Action 1.1:** Inventory of modern architectural heritage – *Completed*.
  - **Action 1.2:** Inventory of religious and institutional architectural heritage – *Completed*.
  - **Action 1.3:** Study to identify archaeological interest sectors on Laval’s territory and define regulatory provisions for works in those sectors – *In progress*.
  - **Action 1.4:** Study on the agricultural and horticultural history of Île Jésus – *In progress*.
  - **Action 1.5:** Fundamental research in history and heritage on still undocumented neighbourhoods – *In progress*.
  - **Action 1.6:** Inventory of historical figures who influenced the development of Île Jésus – *In progress*.

## Intended Use Cases
- Discover municipal **policies and strategic plans** for Laval (service standards, aging, heritage).  
- Access **official policy documents** and action plans for research, civic engagement, or academic work.  
- Use **address-based lookup** for residents to obtain local service information (schedules, locations, elected representative).  
- Support context and documentation needs when working with any associated open datasets from the Laval municipal portal.
