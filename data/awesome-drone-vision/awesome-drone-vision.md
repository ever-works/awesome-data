## Overview

Awesome Drone Vision provides comprehensive resources for developing computer vision systems for drones and unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs).

## Categories Covered

- **Object Detection** - Detecting objects from aerial imagery
- **Tracking** - Visual tracking from drone perspective
- **Navigation** - Vision-based navigation and localization
- **Mapping** - Creating maps from drone imagery
- **3D Reconstruction** - Building 3D models from aerial views

## Datasets Included

- The UZH-FPV Drone Racing Dataset
- The Blackbird Dataset
- VisDrone dataset for object detection
- Stanford Drone Dataset
- Aerial imagery datasets
