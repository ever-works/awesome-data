## Overview
Awesome Deep Learning is a curated directory of deep learning resources, covering educational material, research, software frameworks, tools, and community links focused on neural networks and deep learning.

## Features
- **Curated Resource List**
  - Focused on deep learning and neural networks
  - Includes tutorials, projects, and community resources

- **Books**
  - Collection of books on deep learning theory, practice, and applications

- **Courses**
  - Links to online courses and structured learning programs in deep learning

- **Videos and Lectures**
  - Recorded lectures, talks, and video series on deep learning topics

- **Papers**

- **Tutorials**
