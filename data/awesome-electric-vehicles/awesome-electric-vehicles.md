## Overview

Awesome Electric Vehicles is a comprehensive resource for electric vehicle enthusiasts, covering EV technology, battery systems, charging infrastructure, vehicle models, and the transition to sustainable transportation.

## Key Categories

- **EV Models** - Current and upcoming electric vehicles
- **Battery Technology** - Li-ion, solid-state, and emerging technologies
- **Charging Infrastructure** - Charger types, networks, and standards
- **Powertrain** - Electric motors, inverters, and drive systems
- **Energy Management** - Battery management systems (BMS)
- **Sustainability** - Environmental impact and lifecycle analysis
- **DIY & Conversion** - Electric vehicle conversion projects

## Battery Technology

### Current Technology
- **Lithium-ion** - Current standard for EVs
- **NMC** - Nickel Manganese Cobalt
- **LFP** - Lithium Iron Phosphate
- **NCA** - Nickel Cobalt Aluminum

### Emerging Technology
- **Solid-state batteries** - Higher energy density, safer
- **Lithium-metal** - Improved performance
- **Sodium-ion** - Lower cost alternative
- **Graphene-based** - Faster charging

### Battery Management
- State of Charge (SoC) estimation
- State of Health (SoH) monitoring
- Thermal management systems
- Cell balancing techniques

## Charging Infrastructure

### Charging Levels
- **Level 1** - 120V AC, slow charging (2-5 miles/hour)
- **Level 2** - 240V AC, home/workplace (10-60 miles/hour)
- **DC Fast Charging** - 400-800V DC (100-200+ miles/hour)

### Charging Standards
- **CCS** - Combined Charging System (North America, Europe)
- **CHAdeMO** - Japanese standard
- **Tesla Supercharger** - Proprietary (opening to other brands)
- **GB/T** - Chinese standard

### Charging Networks
- Tesla Supercharger Network
- Electrify America
- ChargePoint
- EVgo
- Ionity (Europe)
