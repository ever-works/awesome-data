## Overview

AngelList (now Wellfound) is the leading platform for startup jobs, connecting talent with venture-backed startups and early-stage tech companies.

## Key Features

- Startup-focused jobs
- Equity information
- Company funding details
- Direct founder messaging
- Startup stage info
- Remote and on-site roles

## Unique Advantages

- **Equity Transparency**: See stock options
- **Funding Info**: Know company stage
- **Direct Access**: Message founders
- **Startup Culture**: Tech-forward companies
- **Growth Opportunities**: Join early, grow fast

## Job Types

- Engineering roles
- Product positions
- Design jobs
- Growth and marketing
- Operations
- Sales

## Company Stages

- Seed stage
- Series A-D
- Growth stage
- Pre-IPO

## Benefits

- Free for candidates
- Rich company data
- Startup insights
- Equity-focused
- Direct communication

## Target Audience

Tech professionals wanting to join high-growth startups with equity compensation.

## Pricing

Free for job seekers.
