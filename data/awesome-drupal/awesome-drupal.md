# Awesome Drupal

**Description**  
Awesome Drupal is a curated "awesome list" of useful resources for the Drupal CMS, including modules, tools, documentation, and community links. Drupal is a free and open source PHP-based content management system used for sites ranging from personal blogs to large corporate, political, and government websites.

## Features

- **Curated tools list**  
  Collection of tools that support Drupal development, deployment, and maintenance.

- **Module references**  
  References to notable Drupal modules that extend core functionality.

- **Documentation resources**  
  Links to official and community-driven Drupal documentation.

- **Articles**  
  Curated articles for learning Drupal concepts, best practices, and implementation patterns.

- **Distributions**  
  References to Drupal distributions (pre-packaged site builds for specific use cases).

- **Books**  
  List of recommended books for learning Drupal and deepening expertise.

- **Videos**  
  Educational and tutorial videos related to Drupal.

- **Podcasts**  
  Audio resources focused on Drupal news, development, and community topics.

- **Community links**  
  Pointers to Drupal community spaces and channels, including:
  - `@drupal`
  - `#drupal`
  - `#drupal-contribute`
  - `#drupal-support`

- **Tips section**  
  General tips and best practices for working with Drupal.

- **Contribution guidelines**  
  Public contribution process with a dedicated `code-of-conduct` and `contributing` documentation to keep the list up-to-date and community-maintained.

- **License**  
  The list is released under the CC0 Public Domain Dedication, allowing reuse and adaptation without restrictions.
