## Overview

Awesome PyTorch List provides a comprehensive collection of PyTorch resources including models, implementations, libraries, and tutorials.

## Categories Covered

- **Models & Implementations**
  - Computer vision models (ResNet, VGG, YOLO)
  - NLP models (BERT, GPT, transformers)
  - GANs and generative models
  - Reinforcement learning algorithms

- **Helper Libraries**
  - PyTorch Lightning - High-level interface
  - Ignite - Training library
  - fastai - Simplified deep learning
  - Torchvision, Torchaudio, Torchtext

- **Tutorials & Courses**
  - Official PyTorch tutorials
  - Community-created learning resources
  - Video courses and lectures
  - Hands-on examples
