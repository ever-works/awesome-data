# Services Engineering

**URL:** https://github.com/mmcgrana/services-engineering#readme  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** services, architecture, devops

## Overview
Services Engineering is a curated, GitHub-hosted collection of resources focused on the design, deployment, and operation of network services. It serves as a knowledge directory for engineers working on service-oriented systems and infrastructure.

## Features
- **Curated resource collection** – Hand-picked links and references rather than raw search results.
- **Focus on services engineering** – Emphasis on the end-to-end lifecycle of networked services.
- **Covers design aspects** – Resources for designing service architectures and interactions.
- **Covers deployment topics** – Materials related to deploying services reliably in different environments.
- **Covers operations and maintenance** – Content about running, monitoring, and maintaining services in production.
- **Developer- and practitioner-oriented** – Oriented toward software and DevOps engineers building and operating services.
