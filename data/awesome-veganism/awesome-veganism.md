# Awesome Veganism

**URL:** https://github.com/sdassow/awesome-veganism#readme  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** awesome-lists, lifestyle, health

## Overview
Awesome Veganism is a community-driven, curated directory of resources, guides, and tools focused on living a fully plant-based, vegan lifestyle. It aims to provide no‑nonsense, 100% vegan‑aligned information to make veganism easier and more accessible.

## Features
- **Curated awesome list**
  - Centralized collection of vegan-related links, tools, and tips.
  - Part of the broader "Awesome" ecosystem of curated lists.

- **Strict vegan focus**
  - All entries are aligned with a fully plant-based lifestyle.
  - Emphasis on avoiding animal products as much as possible.

- **Resource categories** (as defined in the list)
  - **Books** – Reading material related to veganism and plant-based living.
  - **Shoes** – Vegan-friendly footwear options.
  - **Bakeries** –
    - Strictly limited to fully vegan / 100% plant-based bakeries.
    - Includes locations in various cities such as Amsterdam, Berlin, Portland (OR), Salt Lake City (UT), and others.
  - **Restaurants** –
    - Strictly limited to fully vegan / 100% plant-based restaurants.
    - Covers multiple cities (e.g., Amsterdam, London, New York, Oakland, Salt Lake City, and several in Germany and the Netherlands).
  - **Shops** – Vegan-related shops (category defined in the list; contents curated in the repository).
  - **Brands** –
    - Strictly limited to fully vegan / 100% plant-based brands.
  - **Websites** – Online resources focused on veganism.

- **Global scope**
  - Highlights vegan bakeries and restaurants in multiple cities and countries (e.g., Netherlands, Germany, UK, USA).

- **Community-driven curation**
  - Open contributions via GitHub.
  - Maintains a **Code of Conduct** and **Contribution Guidelines** to ensure quality and respectful collaboration.

- **Open licensing**
  - Repository includes a `LICENSE.md` file specifying reuse terms.

## Audience
- Individuals exploring or adopting a vegan lifestyle.
- Vegans seeking trustworthy, fully plant-based products, brands, and places to eat.
- Developers and contributors interested in maintaining or extending an open, curated vegan resource list.

## Pricing
- Access to the Awesome Veganism list is **free** (open GitHub repository). There are no pricing plans mentioned.

- **Code of Conduct:** Sets expectations for respectful participation.
- **Contributing:** Clear guidelines for suggesting new entries or improvements.
