## Overview

Awesome NLP Projects celebrates innovative and delightful Natural Language Processing implementations. This list focuses on projects directly connected or useful for NLP that inspire and demonstrate the practical applications of language technology.

## What Makes a Project "Awesome"?

Projects that:
- Solve real problems creatively
- Push boundaries of NLP technology
- Are well-documented and accessible
- Inspire other developers
- Demonstrate practical applications
