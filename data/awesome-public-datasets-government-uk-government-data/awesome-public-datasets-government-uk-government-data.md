# Awesome Public Datasets – Government: U.K. Government Data

**URL:** https://data.gov.uk  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** datasets, government, open-data

## Overview

The U.K. Government Data portal (data.gov.uk) is a national open data platform aggregating datasets from central government, local authorities, and other public bodies. It enables users to find and reuse government-published data to build products, services, and analyses.

## Features

### Data Discovery
- Central search interface for locating open government datasets.
- Aggregates data from multiple public sector bodies (central government, local authorities, and other public bodies).
- Themed browsing by topic to quickly narrow down relevant datasets.

### Topical Categories
Datasets are organized into multiple domains, including:

- **Business and economy**  
  Small businesses, industry, imports, exports, and trade.

- **Crime and justice**  
  Courts, police, prisons, offenders, borders, and immigration.

- **Defence**  
  Armed forces, health and safety, search and rescue.

- **Education**  
  Students, training, qualifications, and the National Curriculum.

- **Environment**  
  Weather, flooding, rivers, air quality, geology, and agriculture.

- **Government**  
  Staff numbers and pay, local councillors, department business plans.

- **Government spending**  
  Includes all payments by government departments over £25,000.

- **Health**  
  Smoking, drugs, alcohol, medicine performance, hospitals.

- **Mapping**  
  Addresses, boundaries, land ownership, aerial photographs, seabed, land terrain.

- **Society**  
  Employment, benefits, household finances, poverty, population.

- **Towns and cities**  
  Housing, urban planning, leisure, waste and energy, consumption.

- **Transport**  
  Airports, roads, freight, electric vehicles, parking, buses, footpaths.

- **Digital service performance**  
  Cost, usage, completion rate, digital take-up, satisfaction.

- **Government reference data**  
  Trusted reference data shared across government departments.

## Pricing

- Access to datasets via data.gov.uk is provided as open government data.  
- No pricing or paid plans are indicated in the provided content.
