## Description

Awesome MQTT is a curated, community-maintained directory of MQTT-related resources, libraries, and tools focused on Internet of Things and related messaging use cases.

## Features

- **Curated MQTT ecosystem listing**  
  A structured, vetted list of MQTT-related software, libraries, and reference material.

- **Protocol focus**  
  Concentrates on MQTT, a lightweight publish/subscribe messaging protocol optimized for high-latency or unreliable networks.

- **IoT and connectivity use cases**  
  Highlights resources applicable to:
  - Internet of Things (IoT)
  - Telemetry
  - Sensor networks
  - Smart metering
  - Home automation
  - Messaging and notification services

- **Organized by category**  
  Resources are grouped into clear sections, including:
  - **Community Resources**
    - Blogs
    - Talks
  - **Broker** (MQTT brokers/servers)
  - **Cloud** (cloud-hosted MQTT services and tools)
  - **Platforms** (broader platforms that support MQTT)
  - **Tools** (utilities and helper tools for MQTT workflows)
  - **Clients** (MQTT client libraries and applications)
  - **Scripting** (MQTT-related scripting tools and integrations)
  - **Interfaces**, with subcategories such as:
    - Makers
    - ESP
    - Firmwares for ESP-based devices (custom firmware to convert ESP8266-based Wi‑Fi devices into MQTT-controlled smart home nodes)
    - Industry
    - Telephony / PBX
    - Operating System integrations
    - Monitoring
    - Location tracking
    - Logging
    - Smart Home Hardware Interfaces
    - Smart Home Integration Software
    - Lighting
    - Home Entertainment
    - Smart Metering
    - Messaging
    - Misc
  - **Visualization & Dashboards**
    - Dedicated section for visualization/dashboard tools and cross-references to related entries under *Platforms* and *Smart Home Integration Software*.
  - **Architecture & Conventions**
    - Resources on MQTT system design, patterns, and conventions.
  - **Security & Encryption**
    - Resources focused on securing MQTT communication and related cryptography/encryption topics.

- **ESP8266 smart device focus**  
  Includes references and firmware options for inexpensive Wi‑Fi smart home devices based on the ESP8266 chip that can be reflashed and controlled via MQTT.

- **Open contribution model**  
  Public contribution guidelines for adding and maintaining entries, enabling ongoing community-driven updates.

- **Open license**  
  Distributed under a CC0 license, allowing free reuse and remixing of the list contents.

## Pricing

- Not applicable. Awesome MQTT is an open, free-to-access curated list on GitHub.
