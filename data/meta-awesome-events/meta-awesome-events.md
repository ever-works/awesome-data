### meta-awesome: Events

**Category:** Meta-directories  
**Slug:** `meta-awesome-events`  
**Source:** [GitHub – meta-awesome: Events](https://github.com/jepemo/meta-awesome/blob/master/doc/events.md)

#### Overview
meta-awesome: Events is a curated meta-directory that aggregates other “awesome” lists related to events. It focuses on collections of tools, libraries, and resources for event-driven systems and event processing.

#### Features
- Curated meta-directory specifically for event-related resources.
- Aggregates multiple “awesome” lists rather than individual projects.
- Covers event-driven systems, event processing tools, and related technologies.
- Organized as part of the broader `meta-awesome` collection of directories-of-directories.
- Hosted on GitHub, making it easy to browse, contribute, and version-control the list.
- Tagged for discoverability as a meta-directory and directory-of-directories.
