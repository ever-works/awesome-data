## Overview

Awesome NFT DeFi focuses on the intersection of Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) and Decentralized Finance (DeFi), showcasing innovative projects that bring DeFi functionality to NFT assets. This includes lending, borrowing, staking, fractionalization, and liquidity solutions for NFTs.

## Features

- **NFT Lending & Borrowing**: Platforms enabling users to use NFTs as collateral for loans
- **NFT-Backed Loans**: Peer-to-peer and pool-based lending protocols
- **NFT AMMs**: Automated market makers specifically designed for NFT trading
- **Fractionalization**: Protocols for splitting NFT ownership into fungible tokens
- **NFT Staking**: Earn rewards by staking NFT assets
- **Liquidity Solutions**: Tools for improving NFT market liquidity
- **Valuation Tools**: NFT price oracles and appraisal mechanisms
