# Awesome Plotters

**Description**  
Awesome Plotters is a curated “awesome list” of code, hardware, and other resources focused on computer-controlled drawing machines, pen plotters, and visual art robots.

**Category**  
- Themed Directories

**Tags**  
- Awesome lists  
- Hardware  
- Graphics

**Website**  
- Source: https://github.com/beardicus/awesome-plotters#readme

---

## Features

### Getting Started
- Introductory resources for newcomers to pen plotters
- Short, focused selection to help users begin working with plotters quickly

### Hardware
- **Plotters**  
  - Information on pen plotters that can be built or purchased  
  - Historical context and references for classic machines  
  - Restoration and repair project resources

- **Motor Controllers**  
  - Hardware options for driving stepper motors  
  - Controllers and related electronics for operating pen plotters

- **Accessories and Adapters**  
  - Cables and serial adapters for connecting plotters  
  - Replacement parts and hardware accessories  
  - Example: DB9 to DB25 serial null modem cable

- **Pens**  
  - Pen adapter designs and mounts  
  - Pen recommendations and configurations for plotting

### Software
- **HPGL**  
  - Resources related to HPGL (Hewlett-Packard Graphics Language), the text-based protocol used by many legacy pen plotters and modern vinyl cutters

- **G-code**  
  - Resources for using G-code with plotters  
  - Focus on small-scale DIY and hobby projects using CNC or 3D-printer style controllers

- **Plotter Control**  
  - Software tools and applications for sending commands to and controlling plotter hardware

- **Vector Creation**  
  - Tools for creating vector artwork from scratch  
  - Utilities for converting other formats into vector shapes suitable for plotting

- **Vector Utilities**  
  - Tools to manipulate, clean up, and optimize vector-based file formats before plotting

- **Fonts**  
  - Single-line vector fonts (engraving fonts) suitable for pen plotting  
  - Example format: `.fnt`

### Inspiration, Instruction, and Research
- Blog posts, articles, and tutorials on pen plotting and visual art robots  
- Galleries and videos showing plotter-generated artwork  
- Broader research and educational materials related to plotters

### Manuals, Ephemera, Papers, and Patents
- **Manuals**  
  - Scanned manuals for various plotter models  
  - Organized alphabetically by company and product name

- **Ephemera**  
  - Vintage advertising and marketing materials related to plotters  
  - Promotional and informational videos

- **Papers**  
  - Academic papers on pen plotters, plotter art, and related technologies

- **Patents**  
  - Patents relevant to plotter mechanisms, technologies, and related systems

- **Archival Hosting**  
  - Many documents hosted via the Internet Archive
