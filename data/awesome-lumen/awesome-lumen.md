## Overview
Awesome Lumen is a curated, community-driven directory of learning and development resources for the Lumen PHP micro‑framework (by Laravel). It aggregates links to educational materials, community content, and third‑party packages related to Lumen.

---

## Features

- **Curated Resource List**
  - Focused specifically on Lumen, the PHP micro‑framework by Laravel.
  - Includes links to books, videos, articles, demos, and other learning materials.

- **Organized Table of Contents**
  - **Essentials** – core introductory and must‑know resources for getting started with Lumen.
  - **Demos** – example projects and demonstration applications built with Lumen.
  - **Tutorials**  
    - **Written** – text‑based guides and walkthroughs.  
    - **Videos** – screencasts and video courses.
  - **Packages and Middleware** – third‑party extensions, libraries, and middleware that enhance or integrate with Lumen.
  - **Made With Lumen** – real‑world applications and projects using Lumen.
  - **Books** – longer‑form educational materials and references.
  - **Community** – links to community spaces, blogs, or social channels around Lumen.
  - **Tips** – practical hints, patterns, and advice for working with Lumen.

- **Open Contribution Model**
  - Public GitHub repository where anyone can propose additions via pull requests.
  - Simple contribution guideline format for new entries:  
    `* [Name](Link) – Description if needed`

- **License & Usage**
  - Released under the **CC0 (Public Domain)** license.
  - Described as "free of known copyright restrictions," allowing broad reuse and sharing of the list contents.
