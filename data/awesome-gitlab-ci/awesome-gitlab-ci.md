## Overview

Awesome GitLab CI is a comprehensive collection of resources for mastering GitLab's built-in CI/CD capabilities. GitLab CI/CD is natively integrated into GitLab, providing seamless automation for testing, building, and deploying applications without requiring separate CI servers.

## Features

- GitLab CI/CD pipeline templates and examples
- Multi-stage pipeline configurations
- Docker and container-based builds
- Kubernetes deployment patterns
- GitLab Runner setup and configuration
- Auto DevOps and GitOps workflows
- Parallel and matrix builds
- Artifacts and caching strategies
- Environment-specific deployments
- Review apps and dynamic environments
- Security scanning integrations (SAST, DAST, dependency scanning)
- Container registry and package management
- GitLab Pages deployment
- Merge request pipelines and approval rules
- Pipeline optimization and performance tuning
- Integration with external services (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Monitoring and observability for pipelines
- Troubleshooting and debugging techniques
- Migration guides from other CI/CD platforms

## Pricing

Free and open-source community resource; GitLab CI/CD is free in GitLab Free tier with paid tiers offering additional features.
