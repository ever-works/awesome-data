# Awesome Pixel Art

**URL:** https://github.com/Siilwyn/awesome-pixel-art#readme  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** design, graphics, games

## Overview
Awesome Pixel Art is a curated directory of links and resources focused on creating and learning pixel art. It collects tutorials, tools, books, community hubs, and inspiration sources for artists working with pixel-level digital graphics.

## Features
- **Curated resource list**
  - Focused specifically on pixel art creation and learning
  - Emphasis on practical links rather than general art resources

- **Tutorials**
  - **Basics:** introductory materials for getting started with pixel art and core techniques
  - **Color:** guidance on color usage, palettes, shading, and related theory in a pixel context
  - **Animation:** resources on animating pixel art, frame planning, and movement

- **Books**
  - Collection of books related to pixel art and closely related topics

- **Inspiration**
  - **Games:** examples of games with notable pixel art for study and inspiration
  - **Videos:** video-based learning and showcases related to pixel art
  - **Other:** additional inspirational references (e.g., artworks, sites, or media) beyond games and videos

- **Community**
  - **Artists:** links to pixel artists to follow or study
  - **Hubs:** community platforms, forums, or gathering places for pixel art enthusiasts

- **Tools**
  - **Editors:** software and editors tailored for creating and editing pixel art
  - **Miscellaneous tools:** supplementary utilities that support pixel art workflows (e.g., palette tools, converters, helpers)

- **Project information**
  - "Awesome"-style list following the Awesome List conventions
  - Contributing guidelines available via `contributing.md`
  - License information provided via `license.md`
