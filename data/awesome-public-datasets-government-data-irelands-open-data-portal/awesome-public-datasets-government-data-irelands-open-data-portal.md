# Awesome Public Datasets – Government Data: Ireland's Open Data Portal

## Overview
Ireland’s Open Data Portal (data.gov.ie) is the national catalog of open datasets from Irish public sector bodies. It promotes innovation and transparency by publishing Irish public sector data in open, free, and reusable formats.

- **Portal name:** Ireland's Open Data Portal (data.gov.ie)
- **Scope:** National open data catalog for Ireland
- **Operator/brand:** Government of Ireland (Open Data Unit, Department of Public Expenditure, NDP Delivery and Reform)
- **Primary URL:** https://data.gov.ie/data
- **Category:** Themed directory – government open data
- **Focus:** Open, reusable public sector datasets from Ireland

## Features
- **National dataset catalog**
  - Central listing of Irish public sector open datasets
  - Data made available in open, free, and reusable formats

- **Theme-based browsing**
  - Datasets are organized "by theme" to help users find relevant data (e.g., transport, heritage, travel, etc.)

- **News and updates / blog**
  - Posts about workshops and new features, including:
    - European Data Portal workshop (bilingual: English and Irish)
    - Announcements about new functionality such as Superset dashboards on data.gov.ie

- **Dashboards and examples**
  - Integration or linkage to interactive dashboards such as the **Active Travel Dashboard** (Smart Dublin) as an example of using open data.

- **Multilingual content elements**
  - Some content and blog posts available in both Irish (Gaeilge) and English.

- **Open Data guidance**
  - FAQ section providing help on:
    - Accessing data
    - Publishing data
    - Understanding open data provided on the portal

- **Technical platform**
  - Powered by **CKAN**, an open-source data portal platform.

- **Compliance and policies**
  - **Cookie & Privacy Policy** page
  - **Accessibility** information page

- **Attribution and partners**
  - Site implementation by **Derilinx** (noted as site developer)
