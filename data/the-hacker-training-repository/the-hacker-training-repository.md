## Overview

The Hacker Training Repository is one of the most comprehensive collections of security training materials, maintained by security expert Omar Santos, covering offensive security, defensive techniques, and emerging threats.

## Features

- Ethical hacking methodologies and frameworks
- Bug bounty hunting guides and platforms
- Digital forensics and incident response (DFIR)
- AI security and adversarial machine learning
- Vulnerability research and disclosure
- Exploit development tutorials and tools
- Reverse engineering resources and tools
- CTF (Capture The Flag) challenges and solutions
- Security certifications roadmaps (OSCP, CEH, etc.)
- Hands-on labs and practice environments
