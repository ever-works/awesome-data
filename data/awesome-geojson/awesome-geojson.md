# Awesome GeoJSON

**Description**  
A curated “awesome list” of tools, utilities, and resources for working with GeoJSON (geographic JSON) data.

**Category**  
- Themed Directories

**Tags**  
- Awesome Lists  
- GIS  
- JSON

---

## Features

- **Curated GeoJSON Ecosystem Overview**  
  Aggregates links to GeoJSON-related projects, libraries, and services from across the web.

- **Operations**  
  Section dedicated to tools that operate on GeoJSON (e.g., manipulation, analysis, transformation of GeoJSON geometries and feature collections).

- **Editors & Viewers**  
  Collection of applications and libraries for visually editing and viewing GeoJSON data, including map-based interfaces.

- **Validation**  
  Resources for validating GeoJSON documents to ensure they conform to the GeoJSON specification.

- **Services**  
  Links to web services and APIs that consume, produce, or otherwise work with GeoJSON.

- **Conversion**  
  Tools and utilities for converting between GeoJSON and other geospatial or data formats.

- **Data**  
  Sources and repositories providing GeoJSON datasets.

- **Serialization**  
  Libraries and tools for serializing and deserializing GeoJSON in different programming environments.

- **Resources**  
  Additional learning and reference materials about GeoJSON (e.g., documentation, articles, guides).

- **Open Contribution Model**  
  Public CONTRIBUTING guidelines to accept community additions and improvements to the list.

- **Open Licensing**  
  Published under the [CC0 1.0 Public Domain Dedication](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/), allowing broad reuse of the list contents.
