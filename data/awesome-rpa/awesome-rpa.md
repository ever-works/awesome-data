## Overview

Awesome RPA provides comprehensive resources for Robotic Process Automation, including major platforms, open-source alternatives, learning materials, and best practices for implementing intelligent automation solutions.

## Features

- Major RPA platforms (UiPath, Automation Anywhere, Blue Prism)
- Open-source RPA frameworks and tools
- RPA development best practices and design patterns
- Bot management and orchestration solutions
- AI-powered RPA and intelligent automation
- RPA certification and training resources
- Use cases across industries (finance, healthcare, retail)
- Integration with existing systems and APIs
- Community forums and support channels
- Books, tutorials, and video courses

## Pricing

Free and open-source list; individual tools vary from free to enterprise pricing.
