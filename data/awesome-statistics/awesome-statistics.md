## Overview

Awesome Statistics is an extensive curated collection of statistical resources, maintaining nearly 2000 active links to high-quality statistics materials including textbooks, software, packages, courses, and research papers.

- **Statistical Software** - R, Python, SAS, SPSS, Stata, and other statistical computing platforms
- **Textbooks & Books** - Foundational and advanced statistics texts

- **Research Methods** - Experimental design, sampling, and analysis techniques
- **Specialized Topics** - Bayesian statistics, time series, survival analysis
- **Data Visualization** - Statistical graphics and visualization tools
- **Statistical Packages** - Libraries and packages for various platforms
