# Awesome Public Datasets – Government Data: Jail Deaths in America

**Brand:** Reuters  
**Category:** Themed Directories  
**Tags:** datasets, research, government

## Overview
Awesome Public Datasets lists a government-data resource highlighting Reuters’ investigative work on jail-by-jail mortality across the United States. The entry points to compiled data and visualizations on deaths in local jails—information that is not systematically released by the U.S. government.

## Features
- Coverage of jail deaths across the United States at the facility (jail-by-jail) level.
- Investigative dataset created and compiled by Reuters.
- Includes visualizations of jail mortality patterns.
- Focus on government-related data that is *not* otherwise released by U.S. federal authorities in a consolidated form.
- Suitable as a public data resource for research, policy analysis, and investigative work on incarceration and mortality.

## Use Cases
- Academic and policy research on incarceration, public health, and criminal justice.
- Data journalism and investigative reporting on local jail conditions and outcomes.
- Government and advocacy analysis of jail oversight, accountability, and reform.
