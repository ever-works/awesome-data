## Overview

## Benefits Demonstrated

- **Performance**: Near-native speed for compute-intensive tasks
- **Portability**: Run anywhere WebAssembly is supported
- **Security**: Sandboxed execution environment
- **Code Reuse**: Existing C/C++/Rust codebases
- **Size**: Often smaller than equivalent JavaScript
