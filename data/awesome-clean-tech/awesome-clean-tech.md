## Overview

Awesome Clean Tech is a community-driven collection gathering companies and technologies working to combat climate change through clean technology solutions, defined as any process, product, or service that reduces negative environmental impacts.

## Features

- Renewable energy companies and technologies (solar, wind, hydro)
- Energy storage and battery technology innovations
- Electric vehicles and sustainable transportation
- Carbon capture and removal technologies
- Sustainable agriculture and food systems
- Circular economy and waste reduction solutions
- Green building and construction materials
- Climate tech startups and scale-ups
- Research papers and reports on climate solutions
- Job opportunities in the clean tech sector

## Pricing
