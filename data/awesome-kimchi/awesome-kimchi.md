## Overview

Awesome Kimchi is a GitHub repository that aims to archive all types of kimchi "for the people, by the people, of the people" with the tagline "Kimchi of the people, by the people, for the people, shall not perish from the earth."

## Features

- **Comprehensive Kimchi Varieties**: Documents various types including white kimchi (without chili pepper powder), pickled mustard leaves and stems, and Kkakdugi (using Korean white radish)
- **Cultural Preservation**: Archives traditional Korean fermented food recipes and variations
- **Community-Driven**: Open for contributions from kimchi enthusiasts worldwide
- **Educational Resource**: Information about health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, antioxidant, anticancer, and probiotic properties

## About Kimchi

Kimchi is one of the most popular Korean traditional fermented foods, possessing numerous health benefits including anti-obesity properties, cholesterol reduction, and anti-aging effects. This repository celebrates the diversity and cultural significance of this traditional food.
