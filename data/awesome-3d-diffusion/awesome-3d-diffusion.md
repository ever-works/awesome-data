## Overview

Collection of research papers on diffusion models for 3D content generation, covering various aspects from text-to-3D to texture synthesis.

## Research Areas

### Text-to-3D

- DreamFusion approach
- Score distillation sampling
- Multi-view diffusion

### Image-to-3D

- Single-view reconstruction
- Novel view synthesis
- 3D lifting from 2D

### Texture Generation

- UV map generation
- Material synthesis
- PBR textures

### Mesh Generation

- Neural mesh generation
- Topology optimization
- Adaptive refinement

## Key Techniques

- Denoising diffusion
- Latent diffusion
- Conditional generation
- Multi-modal conditioning

## Applications

- Game asset creation
- Product visualization
- Digital content creation
- AR/VR applications
