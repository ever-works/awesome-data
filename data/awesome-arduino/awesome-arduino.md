## Overview

Awesome Arduino is a comprehensive collection of Arduino resources including hardware boards, software libraries, development tools, and project ideas for the popular open-source electronics platform used by makers and embedded systems developers worldwide.

## Arduino Boards

### Official Boards
- **Arduino Uno** - Most popular Arduino board (ATmega328P)
- **Arduino Mega 2560** - More I/O pins and memory (ATmega2560)
- **Arduino Nano** - Small form factor (ATmega328P)
- **Arduino Leonardo** - Built-in USB (ATmega32u4)
- **Arduino Due** - 32-bit ARM processor
- **Arduino MKR** series - IoT-focused boards
- **Arduino Nano 33 IoT** - WiFi and Bluetooth

### Compatible Boards
- **ESP8266** - WiFi-enabled microcontroller
- **ESP32** - WiFi and Bluetooth with dual-core
- **STM32** - Powerful ARM Cortex-M boards
- **Teensy** - High-performance Arduino-compatible
