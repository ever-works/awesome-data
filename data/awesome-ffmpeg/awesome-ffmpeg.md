# Awesome FFmpeg

**Category:** Themed Directories

## Overview
Awesome FFmpeg is a curated, community-maintained directory of resources related to FFmpeg, a cross-platform solution for recording, converting, and streaming audio and video. The list aggregates documentation, tools, libraries, tutorials, and community links to help users work more effectively with FFmpeg.

## Features
- **Curated FFmpeg Resource Directory**  
  - Collects and organizes links specifically focused on FFmpeg and its ecosystem.

- **Coverage of Multiple Resource Types**  
  - **Docs:** Highlights reference and learning materials related to FFmpeg usage and capabilities.
  - **JavaScript:** Section reserved for JavaScript-based tools, bindings, or libraries that interact with FFmpeg.
  - **Native:** Section reserved for native (e.g., C/C++ or system-level) tools and integrations leveraging FFmpeg.
  - **Mobile:** Section reserved for mobile-related FFmpeg tools, wrappers, or SDKs.
  - **Tutorials:** Section for guides and how‑tos to help users learn FFmpeg workflows and commands.
  - **Community:** Section for community resources, such as discussion channels, forums, or groups around FFmpeg.

- **Beginner-Aware Orientation**  
  - Acknowledges that official FFmpeg documentation can be difficult for beginners and positions the list as a way to surface more approachable references and examples.

- **Open Contribution Model**  
  - Public contributing guidelines to allow anyone to propose new entries or improvements to the directory.

- **Open Content License**  
  - Licensed under CC0 (public domain dedication), allowing reuse and adaptation of the list’s contents.
