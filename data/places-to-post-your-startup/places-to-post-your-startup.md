# Places to Post Your Startup

An open, curated directory of online communities and platforms where founders can post and promote their startups, originally compiled from Hacker News threads and maintained as a public GitHub repository.

## Overview
- **Type:** Open-source curated directory / awesome list
- **Focus:** Startup promotion, finding beta users, early marketing
- **Format:** GitHub repository mirrored to a dedicated website
- **Source:** Compiled from “Ask HN: Where can I post my startup to get beta users?” and related HN posts

## Features
- **Curated link collection**
  - List of sites, communities, and platforms where you can post your startup
  - Emphasis on places suitable for finding beta users and early adopters
  - Derived from community-sourced recommendations on Hacker News

- **Category-based organization**
  - **Reddit section:** Dedicated subsection for startup-relevant subreddits
  - **Websites section:** Dedicated subsection for non-Reddit websites and platforms
  - Both sections organized alphabetically for easy scanning

- **Mirrored access points**
  - Primary list maintained in the GitHub repository: `mmccaff/PlacesToPostYourStartup`
  - Automatically mirrored to a standalone site: **PlacesToPostYourStartup.com**

- **Simple navigation**
  - Top-level navigation links to jump directly to:
    - Reddit subreddits
    - Websites
    - License information
  - “Back to Top” links in major sections

- **Open contribution model**
  - Hosted on GitHub, enabling community contributions (issues, pull requests)
  - Maintained as a living list with a commit history

- **Open license**
  - Released under **CC0 (Public Domain Dedication)**
  - Copyright and related rights waived by the author, allowing reuse, copying, and modification without restriction
