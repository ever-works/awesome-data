## Overview

Awesome Sustainability brings together resources for anyone working toward sustainable solutions, from individual contributors to organizations, covering environmental, social, and economic aspects of sustainability.

## Features

- Carbon footprint calculators and tracking tools
- Sustainable web design and green hosting resources
- Energy-efficient coding practices
- E-waste reduction and recycling programs
- Sustainable supply chain management tools
- Green cloud computing options
- Environmental impact assessment frameworks
- Sustainable development goals (SDG) resources
- Community and network opportunities
- Educational materials and courses
