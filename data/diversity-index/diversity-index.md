## Overview

Diversity Index is a curated list of grants, scholarships, travel bursary and financial aid offered to women from around the world by conferences and companies sponsoring education to encourage diversity in STEM fields.

## Features

- **Comprehensive Scholarship Database**: General grants and awards for Women in STEM
- **Post-Doctoral Support**: Research grants and fellowships for graduate studies
- **Conference Funding**: Travel grants and financial aid for attending conferences and meetups
- **Internship Opportunities**: Full-time scholarships (3+ months) for FOSS projects
- **Global Reach**: Opportunities available worldwide regardless of location
- **Diversity Tracking**: Index of women attending or speaking at tech events

## Notable Programs

- ACM-W: Support for undergraduate and graduate women attending research conferences
- GeekFeminism: Travel and attendance funding database
- Various company-sponsored diversity scholarships
- Conference-specific financial aid programs
