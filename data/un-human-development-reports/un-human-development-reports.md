## Overview
The UN Human Development Reports entry in the Awesome Public Datasets collection links to open data from the United Nations Human Development Reports. These datasets provide global indicators on human development and socio‑economic conditions across countries and regions.

## Features
- **Open human development data**
  - Human Development Index (HDI)
  - Related composite indices and metrics
- **Socio‑economic indicators**
  - Education indicators
  - Health and life expectancy metrics
  - Income and economic development indicators
- **Global coverage**
  - Data for countries and world regions
  - Time‑series coverage across multiple years (as provided by UN HDR data portals)
- **Research‑oriented**
  - Suitable for empirical research, policy analysis, and data journalism
  - Standardized indicators widely used in academic and policy work
- **Open access**
  - Publicly accessible datasets for analysis, visualization, and reuse

## Use Cases
- Economic and development research
- Cross‑country socio‑economic comparisons
- Policy evaluation and impact studies
- Teaching datasets for economics, development studies, and social sciences
