# Awesome Heroku

A curated, community-maintained directory of resources, tools, and guides for building, deploying, and operating applications on the Heroku platform.

- **Website**: https://github.com/ianstormtaylor/awesome-heroku#readme
- **Category**: Themed directories
- **Topics**: Heroku, PaaS, cloud, awesome-lists
- **Brand**: Heroku

## Overview

Awesome Heroku is an *awesome list* that aggregates helpful material for developers working with Heroku. It organizes links to documentation, blog posts, tools, plugins, and guides across common Heroku topics such as deployment, architecture, databases, scaling, and more.

This is a resource directory, not a commercial product or service.

### Resource Types
Across the sections, the list includes a variety of resource formats, each explicitly tagged:

- **official** – Primary documentation and guides from Heroku.
- **article** – In-depth writeups, tutorials, or how-tos.
- **blog** – Ongoing blog sources with recurring Heroku content.
- **book** – Longer-form, comprehensive coverage of Heroku-related topics.
- **tool / plugin** – Utilities, add-ons, and plugins that integrate with Heroku.
- **question** – Q&A or troubleshooting discussions (e.g., from forums or Stack Overflow).
- **talk** – Recorded talks or presentations about Heroku or its ecosystem.
- **stunnel / pgbouncer** – Specific tooling references for secure and efficient DB connections.
