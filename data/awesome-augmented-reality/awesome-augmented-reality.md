## Overview

Awesome Augmented Reality provides resources for building AR applications that enhance real-world environments with digital information, supporting mobile, web, and wearable platforms.

## Features

- Mobile AR frameworks (ARKit, ARCore, AR.js)
- Computer vision and image recognition libraries
- 3D object detection and tracking
- Surface detection and plane finding
- Light estimation and realistic rendering
- AR content management systems
- Location-based AR experiences
- Face tracking and facial filters
- Multi-user AR sessions
- AR analytics and performance monitoring
