## Overview

Awesome Virtual Reality catalogs resources for creating immersive VR experiences, from consumer headsets to enterprise applications, covering development, design, and deployment.

## Features

- VR development engines (Unity, Unreal Engine, Godot)
- Platform-specific SDKs (Meta Quest, PSVR2, Valve Index)
- Locomotion and movement systems
- VR interaction patterns and best practices
- Social VR platforms and multiplayer frameworks
- 360-degree video production and playback
- VR training and simulation applications
- Performance optimization for high frame rates
- Motion sickness mitigation techniques
- VR hardware comparisons and reviews
