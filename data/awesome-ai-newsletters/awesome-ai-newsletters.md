## How to Subscribe

### Email Platforms

- **Substack**: Most popular newsletter platform
- **Ghost**: Open-source newsletter platform
- **Revue** (Twitter): Social media integrated (discontinued)
- **ConvertKit**: Creator-focused
- **Buttondown**: Minimalist newsletters

### Aggregators

- **Inbox**: AI newsletter aggregator
- **Matter**: Read-it-later for newsletters
- **Stoop**: Newsletter inbox app

## Tips for Managing Newsletters

- **Dedicated Email**: Separate address for newsletters
- **Filters**: Organize by topic or sender
- **Read Later**: Save to Pocket, Instapaper
- **Unsubscribe**: Regularly clean up subscriptions
- **RSS Alternative**: Some newsletters offer RSS feeds
