### Description
Awesome Blockchain is a curated GitHub list of blockchain-related resources, with a particular focus on blockchain services and decentralized exchange protocols. It aggregates links to articles, tools, libraries, whitepapers, and other reference materials relevant to blockchain and distributed ledger technologies.
