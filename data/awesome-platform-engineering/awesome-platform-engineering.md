## Overview

Awesome Platform Engineering provides comprehensive resources for adopting a developer-centric approach to platform engineering, understanding and building platform teams, automating infrastructure, and improving developer experience through self-service infrastructure.

## Features

- Internal Developer Platform (IDP) design patterns and best practices
- Infrastructure as Code tools and frameworks
- Self-service portals and catalogs (like Backstage, Port)
- Golden paths and paved roads for common use cases
- Platform as Product thinking and methodologies
- Developer productivity metrics and measurement
- CI/CD and deployment automation tools
- Cloud cost management and FinOps resources
- Platform team organization and culture
- Service catalogs and API gateways
