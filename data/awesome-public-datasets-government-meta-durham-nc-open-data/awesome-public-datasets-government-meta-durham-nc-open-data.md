## Overview
Awesome Public Datasets links to the City and County of Durham, North Carolina’s ArcGIS-based open data portal. The portal is a structured directory of government datasets and maps published by Durham for public use.

## Features
- **Government open data directory** for the City and County of Durham, NC
- **ArcGIS-based portal** for accessing geospatial and tabular datasets
- **City and county coverage**, reflecting local government data
- **Includes maps and datasets** (GIS-focused but not limited to maps)
- **Structured catalog** for browsing available public datasets
- **Public access** to government information for analysis, research, and application development
