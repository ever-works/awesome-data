## Overview

Awesome SRE is a comprehensive collection of resources for Site Reliability Engineering, focusing on the practices and tools needed to build and operate large-scale, reliable systems.

## Features

- Culture and organizational practices for SRE teams
- Monitoring and observability tools and best practices
- Incident response and post-mortem templates
- Service Level Objectives (SLOs) and Service Level Indicators (SLIs) guidance
- Capacity planning and performance engineering resources
- Automation and toil reduction strategies
- On-call management and alerting best practices
- Books, papers, and talks from industry leaders
- Training materials and certification paths
