## Overview

Awesome InfoSec focuses on educational resources for learning information security, providing a structured path from beginner to advanced practitioner.

## Features

- Online courses from platforms like Coursera, edX, and Udemy
- Free university courses and lecture series
- Video tutorials and YouTube channels
- Interactive learning platforms (TryHackMe, HackTheBox)
- Information security books and papers
- Podcasts and conferences
- Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions
- Certification preparation materials
- Security awareness training
- Career guidance and job resources

## Pricing

Free and open-source.
