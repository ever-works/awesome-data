## Overview

Awesome Earth Artificial Intelligence collects AI resources specifically for Earth science applications including climate modeling, remote sensing, and environmental monitoring.

## Key Applications

### Climate Science
- Weather prediction
- Climate modeling
- Extreme event detection
- Climate change impact assessment

### Remote Sensing
- Land cover classification
- Change detection
- Object detection in satellite imagery
- Cloud detection and masking

### Environmental Monitoring
- Air quality prediction
- Water quality assessment
- Deforestation detection
- Wildfire prediction

## Data Sources

- Landsat imagery
- Sentinel satellites
- MODIS data
- Climate model outputs
- Weather station networks

## Tools & Frameworks

- TensorFlow for Earth Engine
- PyTorch with geospatial data
- scikit-learn for environmental data
- Deep learning for satellite imagery

## Topics

- Computer vision for Earth observation
- Time series forecasting
- Spatial-temporal modeling
- Transfer learning for remote sensing

## Community

ESIP Federation brings together Earth science and data science communities.
