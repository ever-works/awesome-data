## Overview
Awesome R is a curated directory of R packages, frameworks, tools, and learning resources focused on data analysis, statistics, and visualization. It organizes the R ecosystem into topical sections, helping users quickly discover relevant libraries and references.

## Features

### Curated Yearly Highlights
- Lists of notable R-related developments and resources for specific years (e.g., 2023, 2020, 2019, 2018).

### Development & Programming Environment
- **Integrated Development Environments**: Links to R-focused IDEs and editors.
- **Syntax**: Resources and packages for R language syntax, style, and related enhancements.

### Data Handling & Computation
- **Data Manipulation**: Packages for cleaning, transforming, wrangling, and reshaping data.
- **Database Management**: Tools to connect R with various databases and manage data access.
- **Parallel Computing**: Packages enabling parallel and distributed computing in R.
- **High Performance**: Tools for performance optimization, efficient computation, and large-scale data handling.

### Visualization & Interactive Content

### Reproducibility & Web Integration
